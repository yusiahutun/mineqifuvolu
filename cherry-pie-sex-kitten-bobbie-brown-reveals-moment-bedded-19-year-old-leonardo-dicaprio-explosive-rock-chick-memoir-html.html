February 2024 · 5 minute read

Bobbie Brown - the sex kitten who rose to fame in Warrant's Cherry Pie music video - seduced Leonardo DiCaprio among a host of rock's top names, she claims in an explosive tell all book.

The Inception star was just 19 when he bedded Brown, then 26. 

Brown, who stars in reality show Ex-Wives of Rock, makes the revelation in her forthcoming 90s sex, drugs and rock memoir ‘Dirty Rocker Boys’.

She reveals that she was astonished at the size of his manhood but it was all a bit of a let down because Brown stormed out in a huff because he made her feel strange because he was seven years her junior.

Brown became famous across the world for her sexy performance in Cherry Pie and went on to marry Warrant's lead singer, Jani Lane.

She writes: 'I smile at him (Jani), then step in front of the wind machine, cameras and crew. I feel the heat of the stage lights on me, and there’s a radio playing in the background: “She’s my cherry pie, cool drink of water, such a sweet surprise, tastes so good, make a grown man cry.” Every eye in the room is on me, and I love it.

'I’ve gone from the restless girl in Baton Rouge, La., stuffing her bra with her mom’s socks, swooning over posters of rock stars plastered all over my bedroom wall, to a grown-up 22-year-old making those same rock stars’ heads turn.'

It details her life on the edge partying with some of the most wild men in rock including Motley Crue drummer Tommy Lee, who she nearly married.

At the time she was building up her career as a film star and appeared in a string of music videos, adverts and TV shows like ‘Married With Children’.

Brown, now 44, writes that her relationship with Lee was so up and down that in the space of just four days in 1995 she went from being engaged to him to seeing him marry Baywatch star Pamela Anderson in Mexico.

Angry and newly single, she went on a string of dates including Kevin Costner - but she didn;t sleep with him because she threw a lit cigarette into his bedroom and nearly burned down his house.

She met Stephen Dorff in a club and had to turn him down after being appalled by his opening line: ‘So, you wanna go back to my house and f***?’

It was DiCaprio, however, with whom she nearly went all the way - a year before he became a megastar in Baz Luhrmann's 'Romeo and Juliet'.

They met in the VIP lounge of the Grand Ville club in Hollywood and Brown felt that her ‘inner GILF was ready to party’ so invited him to her house.

They put on TLC’s ‘Waterfalls’, started kissing and undressing themselves on her bed.

Brown writes: ‘I unbuttoned his jeans and tugged down on his boxers. When I saw made me gasp. It made no sense. The kid put Tommy Lee to shame’.

Then DiCaprio dropped the question which almost killed the mood, she claims. ‘So Bobbie, do you have any diseases? Also what about gonorrhea? Have you been tested for that?’

Brown writes: ‘Waves of satisfaction rippled through my body. If only Tommy Lee could see me now. He was a unicorn. Rare, innocent, and horny. Me, on the other hand, I’d been engaged, married and had given birth. I needed a man, not a man-child’.

Brown writes that she first met Lee in 1994, by which time she already had a daughter, Taylar, with her ex-husband, rocker Jani Lane.

She had had a crush on Lee since she was a teenager and couldn’t believe that he took an interest in her.

They fell in love almost instantly and moved in together in an $8,000 a month Malibu mansion.

Lee had four cars, bought Brown a $15,000 bed on a whim, splashed out on Ferraris whenever he wanted and lavished his new love with gifts like $10,000 leather trousers.

But Brown could never help shake the feeling that Lee was cheating - not least because he was always flirting with other women.

Things came to head in 1995 when Lee threw a bottle of red wine at her during a furious row with Taylar in the room.

Lee left and, unbeknownst to Brown, flew to Mexico where he taunted Brown with a brutal voicemail message which went: ‘I’m in Cancun with Pamela Anderson. We have sex toys.'

Brown later found out that when she and Lee broke up, he called Anderson right away, went straight to her in Cancun and immediately proposed.

In her memoir Brown also tells of her long battle with drugs and says that she stayed up for four days on end using crystal meth and speed.

She also abused the latter to slim down for photo shoots and says that she shed 15lbs in a week to do some bikini modeling.

Even years later she was still sore about Anderson and after seeing her and her friends laughing during a party at the Playboy Mansion, she went over and said: ‘Grow the f*** up’.
