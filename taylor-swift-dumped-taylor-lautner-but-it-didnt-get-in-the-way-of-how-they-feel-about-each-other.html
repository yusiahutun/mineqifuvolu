Taylor Swift dumped Taylor Lautner but it didn't get in the way of how they feel about each other

April 2024 · 4 minute read

Taylor Swift and Lautner’s sweet love story that fans have pried over with her song Back to December. The couple may have gone their separate ways years ago, but their continued support and praise for each other is heartwarming to many listeners. Now, years after the breakup, Taylor Lautner has confessed Swift dumped him when it came to the end.

The Twilight star Taylor Lautner is known to be a Swiftie along with wife Tay Lautner. Yes, they all have the first same name, so it gets confusing. The duo attended Taylor Swift‘s Eras tour this year and even had a special moment with the music star. While both Swift and Taylor Lautner have found love elsewhere, the Hollywood star has opened up over their breakup.

Taylor Lautner on being dumped by Swift

Taylor Swift has dated a slew of Hollywood heartthrobs. Each time a love story comes to an end, she seems to pen perfect breakup anthems that chronicle stories of her heartbreak. But Back To December had a different story to tell, according to fans.

The 34-year-old celebrity has hinted in the past how the Speak Now album track is an apology to someone she has wronged. It has been an open discussion between Swifties the song is about Taylor Lautner.

When asked about the particular track, Tay Tay has previously said: “Up until now, I haven’t really felt like I really needed to apologize to someone.” She emphasized that changed with this track though, saying: “It’s just necessary.”

Now, Taylor Lautner is ready to talk about the relationship on the Call Her Daddy podcast. He finally squashed doubts over who broke up the relationship, him or T. Swizzle.

He was asked if the singer broke up with him, or the other way around.

Lautner replies: “I’m thinking back on exactly what happened. But no, yeah, she did.”

Swift’s Back To December lyrics – is it about Taylor Lautner?

Swifties have been discussing who could be the person Taylor is singing to in Back To December, they’ve long suspected it was Lautner.

If you read the lyrics of the song, we can see why they felt that way.

The 34-year-old sings:

We small talk, work and the weather
Your guard is up and I know why

Because the last time you saw me
Is still burned in the back of your mind
You gave me roses and I left them there to die

So this is me swallowin’ my pride
Standin’ in front of you sayin’ I’m sorry for that night
And I go back to December all the time
It turns out freedom ain’t nothin’ but missin’ you
Wishin’ I’d realized what I had when you were mine

US Weekly reports, their love affair, which lasted several months, ended in December 2009. The signs are hard to miss.

Taylor Lautner also acknowledged Swift wrote Back To December about him during a Facebook Live in 2016.

The pair remained ‘wonderful friends’ despite fallout

It is not easy to maintain a friendship with one of your exes, but these two stars did.

Both Taylor Swift and Lautner have had nothing but praise for each other after their breakup in 2009. Recently, the 31-year-old celebrity made fans emotional talking about their friendship, which means the world to him.

The actor revealed what happened after the breakup.

The Twilight star said: “We stayed friendly, we would see each other now and then. But we then did fall out of touch and didn’t talk for a while. But I’ve always had the deepest respect for who she is as a person before anything else..

“Honestly I would say one of the greater things to happen in my life over the last year is the rekindling of our friendship,” he added. “Above anything else that she is, she is just a wonderful human. And she’s pretty great to have in your life.”

The feeling is mutual as the Wildest Dream hitmaker also praised the actor on the Eras Tour stage this year. She called out the celebrity couple (Taylor Lautner and wife Tay).

She said: “He and his wife [Taylor Dome] have become some of my closest friends and it’s very convenient because we all share the same first name.”
