Sweden's Princess Sofia's Best-Dressed Moments

April 2024 · 5 minute read

Being a royal and a fashion lover definitely can't be easy. On top of the public forming opinions on every little thing you wear, there are certain *official* rules that come with those special titles. It's why people were shocked when Meghan Markle didn't wear nude stockings. It's why Kate Middleton is always spotted in a structured shift dress. It's also why we're constantly impressed with nearly everything Princess Sofia of Sweden wears. Somehow, some way, she's managed to find the best of both worlds.

While Sofia's outfits definitely meet a certain regal standard (they're typically modest and always elegant), she isn't afraid to incorporate the latest trends, bright colors, or statement-making accessories. And, it's not just other royals that should be taking notes. We, too, have been learning a little something from these looks as well.

Ahead, we've rounded up 30 photos of Princess Sofia that have left us in awe, convincing us she owns the closet of our dreams — and that she might be the best dressed royal in the world.

Early On, It Was All In the Details

Sofia's full-finger ring — which she wore to the 2013 wedding of Princess Madeleine and Christopher O'Neill — is definitely worth a zoom.

She's a Pro at Packing For a Beach Vacation

You can't go wrong with a neutral pair of shorts, a white tank, and a beach-ready hat.

But She Also Rules the Red Carpet

We're still in love with the short-sleeved, sparkly blue dress that she wore to her pre-wedding dinner.

She Knows the Power of a Good Puffer

Technically, this was at the opening of the FIS Nordic World Ski Championships in 2015 and everyone wore the same coat. But Sofia made it look pretty darn cute.

And, Who Can Forget the Royal's Gorgeous Wedding Dress?

The lace! That veil! The train! It was a true princess moment.

She's Been Known to Wear Classic Color Combos

Like when she topped off her black and white outfit with a bold red coat.

Her Gowns Are Always Breathtaking

And always include a little something special, such as sheer and embellished details on top.

We Must Talk About Her Coats

Draping it over her shoulders instead of putting her arms through the sleeves? This princess is ready for Fashion Week!

And Her Headbands

It's entirely possible she pioneered the statement-making, pearl-inclusive trend way back in 2016.

She's Up to Date on the Latest Styles

We see that ruffled top, Sofia!

She Keeps a Handful of Staples In Her Rotation

The royal does enjoy wearing fitted black pants, which have proven to be just as versatile as a pair of jeans.

She Isn't Above Coordinating With Her Husband

Princess Sofia and Prince Carl Philip went with a black and white theme at a Gala Banquet in 2017.

Sometimes, She'll Keep It Simple

When in doubt, do as Sofia does and throw on a light, printed, long-sleeved dress with a pair of beige heels. It works for a variety of occasions (even royal ones).

She Likes to Incorporate Color Into Her Outfits

Rather than wear all black, Sofia slipped on teal heels and carried a multi-colored clutch at UNDP's 2018 Spring Gala.

She's a Layering Pro

Finally — a solution for what to wear over a fancy gown. Sofia bundled up in a collar-less black coat for a formal gathering at the Swedish Academy.

She's Gone Monochromatic

Forget fussing over footwear — the royal's heels were similar in color to her suit, giving things an on-trend twist.

And She's Gone Full Disney Princess, Too

We'd watch a whole movie dedicated to Sofia's beige ballgown.

It's Also Worth Mentioning All of Her Fun Hats

Somehow, a big bow looks sophisticated instead of cheesy on this royal.

She Loves a Fun Print

And no, that doesn't just mean classic florals and polka dots.

Check Out Her Platform Sneakers!

And that untucked shirt! Such a cool, casual ensemble.

She's Reminded Us Not to Overthink Things

Sometimes, it's as simple as matching your sweater to your skirt. Sofia looked so sleek in this baby blue pairing.

And Convinced Us That a Pair of White Pants Are Timeless

Slightly more eye-catching and summer-appropriate than basic black.

Dramatic Sleeve? She's Down

The sleeves of her red gown swept the floor at the Nobel Prize Banquet in 2018, no doubt stealing the spotlight when she entered the room.

Hoop Earrings? She Has a Pair

She used them to punch up her neutral outfit while at the Entrepreneurship Forum conference.

She's Gone Bright and Bold

In 2019, Sofia mixed baby blue and orange when she styled this fitted pencil skirt with her plaid coat.

But She Knows How Elegant Darker Shades Can Be, Too

Her wine-colored accessories gave her all-black outfit a little something extra.

Just Look at Those Velvet Flares!

Quite the daring fashion choice for a royal. We need these in our closet, Sofia!

She Loves a Work Bag

Much like Meghan Markle, this princess is a fan of a large tote.

She Knows There's No Such Thing as Overdoing It

Headband, drop earrings, and a colorful floral dress? Check, check, and check.

She Might Just Be the Best Dressed Royal Out There

Sofia isn't afraid to experiment with style, wear color, and mix it up from time to time. Plus, these balloon sleeves sure are dreamy!
