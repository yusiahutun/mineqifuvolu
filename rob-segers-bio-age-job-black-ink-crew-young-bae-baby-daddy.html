Black Ink Crew: New York on VH1 is not just about Ceaser Emanuel and his baby mama drama, Crystal Torres drama. A lot has happened and still going on at Ceaser’s tattoo shop in Harlem.

The reality TV show has also dramatically covered tattoo artist Young Bae’s story. She has been on Black Ink Crew since season 5, and fans have seen the ins and outs of her life unfold, ever since.

Young Bae is a fan-favorite amongst the cast and in recent times, she had led fans in wondering who her baby’s father is. Find out in the rest of this writing.

Meet Rob Segers, Black Ink Crew Young Bae Baby Daddy

Throughout Young Bae’s time on the show, viewers have seen see her in a relationship with a guy named Rob Segers. The two of them even moved in together, got married, and eventually had a baby named Niko. So, yes the Young Bae’s baby daddy that you all have been wondering about is none other than Rob.

Viewers were first acquainted with Rob in 2019 and Niko had already been born likely in May 2018. The couple was also yet to get married at this point.

“You are the smartest and most hardworking person I’ve ever met and it’s my honor that you are the mother of our child and my soon-to-be wife”, Rob had written a long sweet message to Young on Instagram after Niko came around. In here, Rob had also promised to be Young’s rock as they raised Niko.

By August 2019 and the first talk of divorce started. Young’s mom had flown in from South Korea and Rob had walked out from Niko and Young’s lives (with no explanation) not long ago. (Of course) Young had started thinking about getting a divorce.

Rob never appeared on Black Ink ever since.

The pair had started dating in 2016 and had gotten engaged sometime in 2018. Their special Time Square proposal was even documented in an episode of Black Ink Crew.

Are Black Ink Crew Young Bae And Her Baby Daddy Rob Segers Back Together?

Young Bae had reportedly said that Rob was not in Niko’s life.

Maybe that was the case at one time. But, in recent times, Rob has been frequently showing Niko and him spending time together, on social media. There was even a pic from September 2020 of the whole family together and Niko was with them of course.

Speaking of Young and Rob getting back together, that does not seem to be true. Even Young Bae herself not long ago admitted that their relationship is over and they have been divorced.

Following their separation, Young once also had accused Bob of leaving her all alone in the center of a miscarriage. Hours after the incident aired on Black Crew Rob had taken to his IG story to tell that the matters are being fabricated.
