New Country Albums Coming in 2023

October 2024 · 4 minute read

New country albums from Shania Twain (Queen of Me), Hardy, Luke Combs and Morgan Wallen introduced 2023 but there are just as big of stars dropping new music this fall.

Tim McGraw, Carrie Underwood and Dolly Parton — her rock album! — will share new country albums before the new year. It's a deluxe album for Underwood, but 100 percent new music from McGraw and Parton.

Relative newcomer Ashley McBryde is going to release her second album in two years on Sept. 8. Also, we keep hearing rumors of a new Zach Bryan album. Could it finally come?

Here are the country music albums coming out during the second half of 2023, as well as those already released.
