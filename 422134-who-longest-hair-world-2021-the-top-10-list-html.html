Who has the longest hair in the world as of 2021? The top 10 list

October 2024 · 8 minute read

If you are a woman, you have at some point in her life wanted long flowing hair. Unfortunately, Rapunzel-like hair isn't in the cards for all of us. However, you will be surprised to know that there are men and women from around the world who have managed or somehow mastered the art of keeping the longest hair. So who has the longest hair in the world?

Long hair is considered an object of envy amongst many women, and it is understandable why they would think that way. Hair is a symbol of beauty and most especially for ladies. In some cultures, long hair is associated with fortune, love and longevity. Most ladies would prefer to grow their hair long. However, others get irritated as soon as it grows past their shoulders.

List of people with the longest hair in the world

On average, human hair grows 6 inches per year, meaning that it would take five long years to grow your dream waist-long hair. Around two-thirds of women have hair that measures longer than 1.4 metres long. Some even possess hair that is more than 5 metres long. I guess you would want to know who has the longest hair in the world. Here is the list of people with long hair.

1. Asha Mandela - 5.9 m

Asha has held the world record for having the longest dreadlocks in the world since 2008. Mandela first started growing her locks in her late 20s, after she moved from Trinidad-Tobago to New York and thought of having a more natural hairstyle. The length of her dreadlocks is 19 feet 6 inches or 5.9 metres.

Maintaining the hair isn’t easy for Mandela. Washing and cleaning it requires a bathtub, and it weighs an estimated 25 to 30 pounds. In order to maintain her locks, she has to wash them once a week, using up to six bottles of shampoo for one wash.

She often carries her hair in a handbag to stop it from trailing on the floor, and when she wears them loose, all eyes are definitely on her. After 25 years of growth, her hair has really become part of her and she feels that if she ever cut her hair she would really be taking away her life.

2. Xie Qiuping- 5.62m

Xie Qiuping from China is the current holder of the title of the longest hair in the Guinness Book of World Records as of 2021. She has held the Guinness world record since 2004, when her hair was officially measured. Xie also has the straightest hair in the world. The length of her hair is 5.62 metres or 18 feet 5 inches.

Xie has been growing her hair since 1973, when she was 13 years old. The average person washes his hair for 20 to 30 minutes at most. However, for Xie, the usual procedure of hair washing takes a few hours, a lot of energy and a bottle of shampoo and conditioner.

3. Dai Yue Qin - 4.2m

Dai Yue Qin, better known as Miss Dyq, is the queen of long hair. Qin also has the straightest hair in the world. She was born in 1964 in South China and began to grow her long hair at the age of 14. Everybody admired her hair, and that motivated her not to cut it off.

4. Ram Singh Chauhan - 4.29 m

5. Ni Linmei - 3.6 m

Ni from China’s Shanxi province has hair that’s twice as long as she is tall. She hasn’t cut her hair since 1998. The hair has grown to about 11 feet, 9 inches or 3.58 metres. Taking care of such long long hair is a daunting task.

She spends two hours each day washing and drying her hair. She probably has to grow her hair for a few more years if she really wants to enter into the Guinness Book of World Records. She might spend a fortune on shampoo, but this Chinese mother-of-two is proud of her 11 feet long hair.

6. Nilanshi Patel - 2m

Nilanshi from Gujarat won the Guinness Book of Records title for having the longest hair ever on a teenager in 2018. At that time, she was only 16 years old, and her hair measured 1.7m. However, just before her 18th birthday, her hair was measured, and it was 200 cm.

8. Keito Kawahara - 1.54m

Keito Kawahara is from Japan. In 2018, Guinness World Records recognized Kawahara as having the longest hair of all teenagers aged between 13 and 17 years old. The length of her hair was 5 feet 1 inch when it was measured in 2018. Unfortunately, she had it trimmed. According to Keito, she was already considering cutting her hair as early as March 2018, just as her record was confirmed.

9. Pelin Ozdemir - 1.52m

Everyone calls Pelin' Rapunzel', and she likes the nickname. The 12-year-old made it to the children's Guinness book of records for having 1.52m long hair. She was rejected by Guinness World Records as she lacked other attributes like age, but she managed to secure a certificate for the kids’ record.

Her parents did not want her to get her hair cut when she was a toddler and then, she made the decision not to cut it herself when she grew up. Pelin says that she never uses any special hair products for her hair to grow. Her mum combs her hair, and this takes around 2 hours to dry and comb.
