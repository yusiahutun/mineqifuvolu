April 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Arsenal fans are plotting to unveil a giant banner of a naked Sol Campbell during their next match against Tottenham.

The Gunners faithful have teased their north London rivals over the signing of the former defender for years.

The ex-Spurs captain infamously left the club for Arsenal on a free transfer in one of the more controversial transfers in recent history.

The two north London sides despise one another and is one of the biggest derbies in the country.

Players for both clubs are far and few between and even less make the trip across town to join the other.

So when Campbell ran down his contract in 2001 to move to Arsene Wenger's side, it ruffled some feathers.

To make matters worse, the ex-England international declared that he would sign a new contract at Tottenham.

But instead, he was paraded as a new Arsenal signing in a shock press conference.

Campbell went on to make 197 appearances for the Gunners, 118 less than what he made at Spurs.

However, his time under Wenger was much more successful, helping Arsenal to two FA Cups and two Premier League trophies.
