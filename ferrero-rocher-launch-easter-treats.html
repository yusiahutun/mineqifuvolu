Ferrero Rocher transform their truffles into mini eggs for Easter

April 2024 · 2 minute read

We've seen lots of fun foodie delights hitting the shelves this Easter, from Aldi's mammoth magnum of prosecco to Waitrose's sold-out Chocolate Avocado Egg - and now it's the turn of much-loved chocolatiers Ferrero Rocher, who have launched adorable mini egg-shaped versions of their cult praline truffles. Did somebody say yum? There's two flavours to choose from - cocoa or hazelnut - complete with the signature Ferrero wafer shell and chocolate coating. Better get to a supermarket quick, because they're selling out everywhere…

Ferrero Rocher's chocolate mini eggs

This isn't the only product that's had an Easter makeover from the brand, as there's even a full-sized egg on offer, though sadly this time it isn't filled with the delicious Ferrero praline mixture. There's plenty of hazelnut flavour in the chocolate shell, though, which is studded with nutty pieces. 

The traditional Ferrero Rocher Easter egg is proving popular, too

But if nothing beats the original for you, the Ferrero Grand Rocher would be your best bet - it's essentially a giant version of the sugary treat! Wrapped in the iconic packaging with a big green bow on top, it would make the ideal egg-alternative for any Ferrero Rocher fanatic, as inside is a super-sized milk chocolate and hazelnut shell, with four signature truffles inside. 

This giant Ferrero Rocher is the ultimate treat

Ferrero Rocher aren't the only chocoholics doing something different with their Easter offering - luxury department store Selfridges will be selling an Easter egg filled with gelato, while Cadbury have created a ginormous 45kg egg for the occasion, which apparently contains the equivalent of 1,111 standard Dairy Milk bars! Wow.
