Who Has a Higher Net Worth Lady Gaga or Adam Driver?

June 2024 · 3 minute read

Lady Gaga is the chart-topping artist behind songs like “Born This Way,” “Rain On Me,” and “Bad Romance.” Adam Driver is known for his role as Kylo Ren in Star Wars productions like The Rise of Skywalker. 

They seem like an unlikely pair. Together, these actors starred in the 2021 release House of Gucci. Here’s what we know about their respective net worths.

Lady Gaga and Adam Driver star in the crime drama, ‘House of Gucci’

House of Gucci is the story of wealth, power, fashion, and, eventually, murder. The film also stars Al Pacino, Jared Leto, Salma Hayek, and others, with Lady Gaga and Driver portraying the main characters, Patrizia Reggiani and Maurizio Gucci respectively.

This is the first project Lady Gaga and Driver worked on together, with both celebrities leading different acting careers. Lady Gaga is known for her musical endeavors, recently releasing her Chromatica and Dawn of Chromatica albums.

Who has a higher net worth — Lady Gaga or Adam Driver?

These artists both starred in several films. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Driver has an estimated net worth of $16 million. That’s thanks to his work on the HBO drama series Girls, as well as his role in the Star Wars sequel trilogy and award-winning productions like BlacKkKlansman and Netflix’s Marriage Story.

Celebrity Net Worth reports that Lady Gaga has an estimated $320 million, some of which is due to her Las Vegas residency, album sales, tours, and stardom in productions like A Star is Born. That means Lady Gaga has a higher net worth when compared to her House of Gucci co-star.

Both actors are deeply involved with separate philanthropic projects. For Driver, that’s Arts in the Armed Forces. The nonprofit organization arranges theatre performances for all of the branches of military personnel in the U.S. and abroad.

For Lady Gaga, that’s the Born This Way Foundation. According to their website’s description, it is a nonprofit “committed to supporting the wellness of young people, and empowering them to create a kinder and braver world.”

Although these artists live pretty different lives, they often share praise and admiration for one another during House of Gucci-related interviews. Lady Gaga even wished her co-star a happy birthday on Instagram. 

“I’m the lucky actress who got to learn from you and lead with you every day,” Lady Gaga captioned an Instagram post. “Shoutout to all your fans! I know why they adore you. It’s [because] you’re the best! (And you’re a weirdo like me).” 

“Chemistry is not something I work on with people,” Driver said during an interview with ET Online. “We don’t sit at a table and ‘now let’s work on chemistry with each other.’ It’s just kind of naturally happens when people are prepared, and they show up, and they’re awesome and available and can play.”
