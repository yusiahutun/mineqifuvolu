Who Is Priscilla Presley? Get to Know Elvis' Former Wife

January 2024 · 3 minute read

Talk about legendary! Priscilla Presley is a seriously impressive woman. The classic beauty is an actress, a business woman, an author and, yes, was formerly married to Elvis Presley.

Even though they split up four years before the iconic singer’s tragic death in 1977, Priscilla didn’t let her feelings for Elvis affect her drive to succeed. She went on to become the chairwoman of Elvis Presley Enterprises and to star in the hit movie The Naked Gun: From the Files of Police Squad! Although her acting career ended in 1999, Priscilla has still made a lot of money from her business ventures with Graceland.

With her help, Graceland quickly became one of the most popular tourist attractions in the United States. So, yes, Priscilla makes a very pretty penny!

Scroll down to learn everything that there is to know about Priscilla! 

Who are Priscilla’s children and grandchildren?

Priscilla and Elvis had one child together: Lisa Marie Presley. She also has a son, Navarone Garibaldi, with her longtime boyfriend Marco Garibaldi. Priscilla has four grandkids via Lisa Marie — Riley Keough and Benjamin Keough as well as twins Finley and Harper Lockwood. When last we heard, the twins live with Priscilla — and she’s completely in love with them!

What is the Love Me Tender book?

In 2017, Priscilla wrote the end note for a book inspired by Elvis’ famous father/daughter ballad “Love Me Tender.” In September 2017 she told People how excited she was for the twins to read the book. “I can’t imagine them not liking it,” she said. “First of all, they will instantly recognize the title and secondly it’s a feel-good story of love and accepting and a happy ending. Isn’t that what children want?”

What are Priscilla’s best memories of Elvis?

Priscilla recalled exclusively to Closer Weekly that Elvis once got her a 1964 Chevrolet Corvair. “Elvis gifted it to me as a [high school] graduation present in 1963,” she explained. “I loved it! Can you imagine? It was my very first car and the fact that he gave it to me made it very special. I remember taking Elvis for a drive in it, but only around the circular driveway at Graceland. He didn’t want to be seen with a girl driving him around!”

How old is Priscilla Presley?

Priscilla was born in Brooklyn on May 24, 1945. The iconic actress is now 74 years old and still looking as beautiful as ever!

Is Priscilla Presley dating?

Priscilla was seen spending time with Tom Jones in February 2017 after the singer’s wife died, so fans quickly assumed they were dating. However, those rumors are untrue, and both Tom’s rep and Priscilla denied them.

What is Priscilla Presley’s net worth?

According to The Richest, Priscilla is estimated to be worth around $150 million, though Celebrity Net Worth puts it at $50 million. From her business ventures to acting endeavors to being the executor of Elvis’ estate and founding Elvis Presley Enterprises, Priscilla has worked hard to become the successful business woman she is today! You go, girl!
