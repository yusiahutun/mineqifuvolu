Margaret and Geoffrey struck up a conversation, which developed into a friendship, and later, their relationship blossomed into romance. Three years after they met, the couple tied the knot at Our Lady of Lebanon Roman Catholic Church, located in Niagara Falls, New York.

Margaret and Geoffrey Zakarian have been married for 17 years, and their relationship is still strong. They have three children together. Their firstborn, Madeline Zakarian, was born in April 2007, with her sister, Anna Zakarian, coming into the world two years later.
