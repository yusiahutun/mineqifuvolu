Doctors' salaries and allowances in Kenya in 2023/2024 revealed

March 2024 Â· 4 minute read

The Kenyan healthcare system is quite diverse, with numerous institutions, healthcare practitioners, and medical insurers. Doctors are an integral part of this system and are tasked with numerous responsibilities touching on preventive, curative, and rehabilitative care. As with other professions, doctors' salaries are determined by various factors, including experience, specialization, and place of work. What is the average doctor's salary in Kenya today?

Generally, experienced doctors earn more than their entry-level counterparts. Additionally, medical practitioners working in large, well-established health facilities also make significantly more than those in small facilities.

What is the average doctor's salary in Kenya?

Here is a look at the average salaries that doctors take home based on various factors.

Salaries based on experience

Doctors can be classified into entry-level, mid-level, and senior level based on their experience. Here is a look at the average salary for each level.

  • Entry-level doctors: These have 0-2 years of experience. They earn an average of Ksh 138,000 per month.
  • Mid-level doctors: These ones have 3-9 years of experience and earn anything between Ksh 142,000 and Ksh 302,000 per month. A doctor's specialization greatly affects their salary at this level.
  • Senior-level doctors: These doctors are renowned for their experience in the medical industry. They tend to have over ten years of experience. Doctors at this level take home over 350,000 per month. The specific figures are also determined by their fields of practice, places of work, and contractual agreements.

Allowances for doctors in Kenya

Besides their monthly salaries, Kenyan doctors also earn a wide range of allowances. These are based on the same factors that determine one's basic salary (experience, place of work, specialization). Here is a look at the average allowances per month.

  • Commuter allowance: Ksh 3,000 to Ksh 9,000
  • Emergency allowance: Ksh. 20,000 to Ksh 50,000
  • Health allowance: Ksh. 10,000 to Ksh 30,000
  • House allowance: Ksh. 10,000 to Ksh 25,000
  • Leave allowance: Ksh. 5,000 to Ksh 15,000
