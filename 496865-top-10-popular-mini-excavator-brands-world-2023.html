1. The Yanmar

The Yanmar ViO35-6A compact excavator is popular due to its zero tail swing capabilities and fuel-efficient Tier 4 engine. It offers 20% less fuel consumption than older models, with auto-deceleration and ECO modes.

In addition, the quick coupler system and easy-access engine compartment increase its efficiency. This model has an 8,214-pound operating weight, 11.25 feet max digging depth, and 24.4 horsepower, making it suitable for navigating compact worksites, handling construction tasks, landscaping, and utility.

2. Kubota

The Kubota mini excavator has been named the "Best Buy" by Farm Show and is ideal for small farm operations. In addition, the Kubota U10-5 highlights suitable features to work in limited spaces. With a weight of 1125kg, 1800 mm max dig depth, 2980 transport, and 750 to 990 overall width, the machine is a must-have for constructors in 2023.

Moreover, Kubota gives an incredible offering inside its ultra-compact frame. Kubota's reputation for operator comfort and durability shines through in the U10-5. Besides an adjustable track range, you'll have access to standard two-speed for increased control while using the machine.

3. Takeuchi

The Takeuchi TB335R boasts a minimal tail swing design that allows operators to focus on their work without worrying about rear swing impacts. In addition, its compact radius provides excellent manoeuvrability and accessibility in confined worksites while maintaining stability.

With features like a 3.76 t weight, 4.89m transport length, and 95.8 Nm max torque, Takeuchi mini excavators enhance the operator's experience with an entirely redesigned interior. The cabin interior is spacious and inviting for enhanced comfort and productivity throughout the day.

Daily inspections and maintenance are accessible with the TB335R's swing-out engine hood and overhead-opening right-side cover. These features allow operators to quickly inspect components such as the water separator, oil cooler, radiator, hydraulic oil level sight gauge, dual element air cleaners, fuel level, coolant level, engine oil dipstick, and battery.

The E35 also has advanced features like automatic shift and hydraulic thumb control, allowing for precise work even in challenging conditions. Overall, the Bobcat E35 remains a top-performing mini excavator in 2023.
