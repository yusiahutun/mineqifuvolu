Arsenal star's 'Twerk Queen' WAG is 'banned' on Instagram after posting bikini picture

February 2024 · 2 minute read

Arsenal defender Jakub Kiwior’s fiancée Claudia ‘Redhead’ Kowalczyk has been ‘banned’ by Instagram after posting a bikini picture.

Claudia has previously been dubbed ‘Twerk Queen’ and it doesn’t take long looking at the uploads on her social media accounts to understand why. She’s built-up a following of over 78,000 followers on Instagram thanks to her bum-shaking choreographies and skimpily-clad outfits.

Known by the stage name ‘Redheaded’, Claudia is a flamboyant dancer and previously reached the semi-finals of Germany’s twerk championships back in 2017. She’s since continued to send pulses racing but it appears her content has been rendered too raunchy and revealing for social media.

Claudia uploaded a picture of her on holiday in Dubai wearing a tiny black bikini while she raised her left arm in the end which hoisted her shirt up, showing off her peachy behind. While her fans lapped it up, with one labelling her “Amazinggg” with a second simply commenting love heart eye emojis.

But she didn’t receive the same amount of love from Instagram, who sent her a message regarding her recent post, which read: “This material cannot be recommended to people who do not follow you”.

Claudia’s warning may mean she has to tone down her gushing videos, which hopefully won’t affect her work which has seen her travel to Dubai earlier this month and will see her in Malaysia early next month for a twerk workshop.

The Poland international was a late substitute during Arsenal’s comfortable 5-0 win against Crystal Palace on Sunday, seeing the Gunners return to winning ways in the Premier League for the first time since December, 17, against Brighton & Hove Albion.
