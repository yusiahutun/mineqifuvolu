20 popular black female YouTubers to watch right now

February 2024 · 8 minute read

Women of colour have not been left behind when it comes to occupying online spaces on platforms like YouTube and Instagram. Fans massively consume the high-quality content they produce all over the world as they share their experiences and lifestyle. Popular black female YouTubers continue to rise and create movements that are influential and help black girls deal with issues that were previously not spoken of.

Most of the famous black YouTubers are into fashion, makeup, hair, skincare, and all things beauty. The mentioned trends seem to attract a considerable following, given that the YouTubers talk about things that their followers use and experience. The YouTubers who have managed to stay on top have always been disciplined and consistent with uploading content.

20 popular black female YouTubers

Subscribers appreciate YouTubers who regularly update their channels with new and fresh videos. That is what makes the crème de la crème of the black girls' beauty community on YouTube. Here are the 20 black female Youtubers:

1. Jackie Aina

Jackie Aina was an American army veteran before she joined the beauty community online. Her fans love her for the fantastic content she produces, which includes self roasts and her outspoken personality. She is one of the most famous black female YouTubers with 3.56 million subscribers and 924 video uploads.

2. Alyssa Forever

Alyssa Forever is a lifestyle YouTuber. She sometimes involves her boyfriend and other close friends in her videos. The beauty queen has 1.33 million subscribers and 96 video uploads. She is a real slayer who inspires many black women.

3. GloZell Green

GloZell Green has established herself as one of the most popular female black YouTubers today. Her channel has 4.66 million subscribers and over 2,300 video uploads. She has been on YouTube since 2008, and her consistency with quality videos has seen her become one of the fastest rising beauty YouTubers.

4. The Glam Twinz

The Glam Twinz is run by identical twin sisters Kelsey and Kendra Murrell. The Georgia-based YouTubers talk about everything hair, makeup, fashion, and beauty influencer space. Their channel has 686k active subscribers and 616 video uploads. Follow them and get to learn about overnight detoxing, hair management, skincare, and their daily routine.

5. Ashley Devona

Ashley Devona is gradually making a name for herself as one of the most sought-after black female YouTubers. Her channel has 390k subscribers and 401 videos. She is not all beauty without brains.

The gorgeous lady is an alumna of Texas Women’s University, with a degree in Business Administration and a concentration in Marketing. Ashley Devona not only talks about beauty but also touches on mental health and the importance of therapy.

6. MakeupShayla

Shayla Mitchell, popularly known as MakeupShayla runs the YouTube channel by the same name. The 33-year-old black beauty YouTuber commands a following of 753k subscribers and has uploaded 307 videos. She shares her journey, experience with beauty products, and bits of her family.

7. MonicaStyle Muse

MonicaStyle Muse is never shy of mentioning her Dominican roots when she is doing her thing. The beautiful Youtuber full name is Monica Veloz. The Brooklyn native brings you everything from fashion, makeup, and keeping fit as you look glamorous. She has about 400k subscribers and 375 video uploads.

8. Patricia Bright

Patricia Bright is one of the most established black British YouTubers today. She is one of the pioneers in her country and has been posting on her YouTube for a long time.

The bubbly YouTuber shares everything about her wardrobe, family, motherhood, fashion, makeup, and everything feminine. She currently has 2.91 million subscribers with 786 video uploads.

9. Ms Aaliyah Jay

Ms Aaliyah Jay invites over 1.5 million subscribers to her life through her daily routine. Do you want to know where to get the most suitable product for your skin? Aaliyah will help you do that as she shares her experiences while purchasing and consuming all sorts of products. Her channel has 355 videos.

10. Shameless Maya

The name of the YouTube channel alone will get anyone interested. Shameless Maya has 1.19 million subscribers and 387 video uploads. The girl YouTuber has proven that with the dedication to one's work, one can grow a substantial online following. The YouTuber has content for days and appeals to both young and older women.

11. Nyma Tang

If you want that melanin popping on your YouTube feed, then you need to subscribe to Nyma Tang's channel. She calls out the prejudice in the beauty industry and speaks up whenever she sees her melanin sisters being victimized.

12. Bri Hall

Bri Hall is here to guide you on how best to apply your make up during all the seasons of the year. The young YouTuber with about 790k subscribers and 298 video uploads shares her makeup and fashion journey. She aims to inspire other black girls and help them be glamorous.

Whitney White runs the popular Naptural85 YouTube channel. The beauty enthusiast is not only involved with creating content for YouTube, but she also runs a successful business. Get updated on the latest hair trends, best makeup to use, and how to be successful in the digital age by following Naptural85. The YouTuber has 1.19 million subscribers and 369 videos.

15. Alissa Ashley

The curly hair queen has managed to amass slightly over two million subscribers since she started her beauty channel. Alissa Ashley has 92 video uploads, but her followers are contented with the low number of videos because her content is nothing short of quality. She brings a new style of selecting and applying makeup every time she uploads a new video.

16. Tiarra Monet

Tiarra Monet is working hard to be on the one million-plus subscriber side. She has 700k subscribers and has uploaded 176 videos that show her in different moods, locations, styles, and the trends she follows. She runs a thriving salon in NYC and has been selected as the Cover Girl ambassador.

18. Cydnee Black

Cydnee Black is another Melanin queen whose videos are not only inspiring but also encourage black girls to love and flaunt their beautiful skin.
