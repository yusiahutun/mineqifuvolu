Taylor Swift Just Wore 50 Shades of Brown

July 2024 · 2 minute read

If Taylor Swift has anything to say about it, monochrome quiet luxury dressing isn't going anywhere, though her latest 'fit features enough Gucci G logos to give her brand of luxury a little more volume. During an outing to Minetta Tavern in New York on Friday — which included a cameo from fellow singer and Jack Antonoff collaborator Phoebe Bridgers, according to TMZ — Swift paired a tan-and-chocolate Gucci logo sweater with similarly earthy-hued flared pants and heeled chunky penny loafers in a deep, warm cocoa color. She carried a camel-colored coat in her arm and had a light tan satchel bag slung over her shoulder, which made us lose count of just how many shades of brown she managed to combine in just one outfit.

Swift had her signature red lip and cat-eye liner on, as well, and had her long blonde hair straight and parted down the middle. 

Swift's pre-weekend outing comes just days after reporters asked her rumored beau, Travis Kelce, whether or not he was in love with the Grammy winner. In true celebrity form, Kelce said that he just wanted to keep his "personal relationship personal" and brought the focus back to football, not falling in love with a pop star that could possibly chronicle their entire relationship with an album in the near future. Kelce and the Kansas City Chiefs are set to play a powder keg match on Sunday against the Miami Dolphins in Frankfurt, Germany. 

"I think it's pretty much a coincidence. I've had some pretty big games in my career," Kelce said when a reporter asked him whether or not he plays better when Swift is in the stands cheering him on. "You know what … I think when I mention or everybody knows that she's at the game, the over/under on my catches kind of goes up and down. The spread goes up and down. So, you know, I don't want to mess with any of that stuff. So I'm just gonna keep it to myself."
