12 of Blackpinks Jennies best-ever Chanel fashion looks: the houses rep wears vintage tees

July 2024 · 4 minute read

Most recently, the fashion icon starred in Chanel’s new fine jewellery campaign released in January alongside Margaret Qualley and Amandla Stenberg, indicating that she’s not going anywhere any time soon.

From a simple vintage tee to handbags galore, we take a look at Jennie’s best-ever Chanel outfits.

1. Vintage tee, July 2018

Back in 2018, Jennie wore this cropped vintage tee from the brand as she strutted down a hallway with fellow bandmate Lisa and Jisoo, who pops up in the background. She paired the ripped turquoise shirt with baggy trousers, giving off a cool girl 90s vibe.

2. Printed scarf top, March 2019

Fast forward a year later and Jennie channelled a peak 2000s look with her printed Chanel scarf top paired with logo earrings from the fashion house.

3. Snow queen, March 2019

One of Jennie’s most iconic looks is this gorgeous winter-wonderland ensemble she wore for Chanel’s autumn/winter 2019 show at Paris Fashion Week. Here, she proved she’s more than just the “YG princess” – she’s also an “ice queen”, as netizens lovingly dubbed her after seeing the snow-set look.

4. Two-toned crochet dress, July 2019

In the summer of 2019, Jennie wore a blue, yellow and white tweed dress from Chanel’s spring/summer 2019 collection – the same look that Johnny Depp’s model daughter and fellow house ambassador Lily-Rose Depp wore at the César Revelations dinner months before.

The chic dress reportedly costs US$5,700.

5. Peekaboo jumpsuit, July 2020

The 27-year-old shone in her signature Cs-emblazoned tank top peeping out under her utilitarian all-white jumpsuit in this July 2020 post.

6. Pink parade, March 2021

Ever the girlie girl, the “Shut Down” singer wore this “strawberry milky” outfit, as she describes it, for a photo shoot in March 2021.

7. Coconeige, October 2021

To celebrate her Coconeige winter campaign envisioned by Chanel’s Virginie Viard, the K-pop idol sported this head-to-toe look, boasting a pricey combination with her US$5,000 logo polo neck, US$2,000 Camellia clutch with chain, US$1,000 lock necklace and a US$7,000 blinging belt.

8. Monogrammed muse, October 2022

A regular fixture at fashion weeks, Jennie attended Chanel’s spring/summer 2023 Paris shows last year in this Cambon-branded one-shoulder knit dress and cardigan showing that she’s truly the walking-and-talking “human Chanel”. It’s unclear how much the clothes cost, but her thigh-high suede boots are estimated to retail for over US$3,000. She was spotted at the event chatting to fellow Chanel ambassador and actress Kristen Stewart.

9. Solo stage, December 2022

If you loved this outfit as much as we did, sadly, it’s not on the market. According to Vogue, the singer’s costume was made specially for her by Chanel’s creative director Viard and the label’s design team. The white strapless bodysuit features a bow tie belt with a see-through lacy skirt and long black gloves to finish the look.

10. Pink for Paris, December 2022

In December last year, The Idol actress stunned in full-on Chanel with her pink rocker-chic look, from the arm and leg warmers to the glittering Mary-Janes, while on another trip to Paris.

11. Tweed galore, January 2023

For a house highly associated with tweed, it only made sense that Jennie would occasionally return to the brand’s roots in her choice of ‘fits. On January 28, she posted this classic ensemble, donning tweed pieces from the fashion label.

It cost a pretty penny too, with her glittered tweed jacket and vest at US$8,600 and US$5,100, respectively, paired with a US$1,800 skirt and matching tweed mini flap bag at US$5,200.

12. Stage goddess, January 2023

Jennie steals the spotlight for her incredible vocal talents, but also for her impeccable fashion. At Le Gala des Pièces Jaunes concert on January 29, the songstress incorporated elements of Chanel in her look, including the Camellia flower motif, with her top, shorts and scarf from the brand’s spring/summer 2023 collection.
