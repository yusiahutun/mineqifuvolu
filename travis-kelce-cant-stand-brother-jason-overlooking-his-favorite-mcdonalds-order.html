Travis Kelce can't stand brother Jason overlooking his favorite McDonald's order

March 2024 · 3 minute read

Travis Kelce has called out his brother Jason over his McDonald’s order on their New Heights podcast.

During a segment on their latest episode, Travis Kelce and his brother discussed McDonald’s and Jason revealed exactly what he gets “every time” he orders from the fast food chain. However, Taylor Swift’s beau seemed almost offended over one item that does not make it onto Jason’s tab. However, Jason, 36, defended his decision and explained why he doesn’t have it.

Travis Kelce is shocked by Jason’s McDonald’s order

During their chat, Jason Kelce revealed exactly what his go-to order is from McDonald’s and his brother Travis was not happy.

“Every time I go there I get two sausage, egg, and cheese [McMuffins] with a large coffee, black,” Jason began, “either hot or iced. It’s always ice or hot, and they know it.”

“No hashbrowns?” asks Travis sounding concerned.

“Dude, I don’t go hashbrowns -” Jason starts only to be cut off by his Kansas City Chiefs-playing brother whose eyebrows shoot up. “What the f*** is wrong with you?!” he asks.

Jason replies: “I don’t do combo meals. If I’m going there to get a burger I get a double quarter pounder with cheese. I don’t go there to get the value number four with the fries and the -“

“That’s the point, it gets you a better deal!” argues Travis.

“That’s a mistake,” says Jason, “Those fries and that drink? You’re not getting your money’s worth on that. It’s not enough calories per hour.”

Travis has his final say in emphasizing it’s got the “perfect crunch” and went as far as to call it the chain’s “best item.” Meanwhile, Jason insisted it was a sausage, egg, and cheese McMuffin.

Jason says Travis’ newfound fame is ‘a lot’

Jason Kelce’s McDonald’s order isn’t the biggest shock the brothers have found themselves in recently. After Travis, 34, began dating music star Taylor, also 34. Jason is said to have told Peter King, a sports journalist, the media attention is “a lot.” But the celebrity did say he was happy for his Travis.

Jason explained in his article: “It’s certainly been weird, the level that it is now. On one hand, I’m happy for my brother that he seems to be in a relationship that he’s excited about, that he is genuine about. But there’s another end of it where it’s like, ‘Man, this is a lot.’”

He continued: “This is another level of stardom that typically football players don’t deal with. And so on one hand, really, really happy for my brother and where he’s at in his current situation with Taylor but on the other hand, there’s some, I think, alarms sometimes with how you know, over-in-pursuit people can be.”

He said as long as everyone stays safe things should be fine as he knows Travis can “deal with” being in the limelight.
