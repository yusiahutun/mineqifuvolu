Patti McGuire is an American model and television producer, worked predominantly in Hollywood. She was a big name of modeling industry during 70s and even mention of her name used to draw fascination. Patti was born on 15 September, 1951 and raised at Dexter, Missouri, United States with her younger brother and two younger sisters.

Miss McGuire who became a sensation in the field of modeling was her High School’s Homecoming Queen also. Before her big break as a model, she juggled at the Playboy Club in St. Louis as a bunny and a student at Southern Illinois University, where she majored in Political.
