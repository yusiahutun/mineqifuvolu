After 14 years of marriage, their divorce was rumoured to have been finalized November 2011.

And David Duchovny and Tea Leoni are still tying up loose ends - finally listing their three-story maisonette in New York's Upper East Side for $9.25 million on January 16.

The 52-year-old actor and the 46-year-old actress originally purchased their lovenest for $6.25 million in 2008 - the same year they first separated allegedly due to Duchovny's sex addiction.

It's all over: David Duchovny and Tea Leoni finally listed their three-story maisonette in New York's Upper East Side for $9.25 million on January 16

The Californication stud then checked into rehab for treatment and the pair reconciled briefly before splitting for good in June 2011.

However, the former Hollywood couple continue to share two children - 12-year-old daughter Madelaine and 10-year-old son Kyd.

According to the home's listing onStribling, the five-bedroom, four-bath residence on East 78th street in Manhattan was once three separate artist studios.

The two-time Golden Globe winner was said to be dating his X-Files co-star Gillian Anderson following her split with longtime partner Mark Griffiths.

But David's representative denied rumours of a romance to Celebuzz last August.

Meanwhile, Tea's last acting role was in the 2011 Brett Ratner comedy Tower Heist.
