Career

Benjamin David Gillies was born on October 24, 1979, in Merewether, a Newcastle suburb. He began as a quad drummer in the band The Marching Koalas before forming the grunge band Innocent Criminals with friends Daniel Johns and Chris Joannou when he was 12 years old. They rose to prominence after a weekend music workshop at the Tanilba Bay Progress Hall in March 1994, which was funded by the Arts Council of New South Wales. They had previously performed at schools and other venues in the Newcastle area.
