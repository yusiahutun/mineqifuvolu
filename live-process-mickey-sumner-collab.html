Live The Process Collaborates On A Spring Line With Actress Mickey Sumner

May 2024 Â· 1 minute read

Lifestyle brand Live The Process knows no bounds when it comes to creating chic, easy activewear pieces for modern health and wellness. That's why it's no surprise that the athleisure brand has taken a philanthropic approach with their next project by teaming up with actress-philanthropist Mickey Sumner and The Lunchbox Fund, an organization that aims to foster education through nutrition in South Africa. With nothing short of excellent detail, the capsule collection is inspired by the South African national flower, protea, and celebrates the vibrant spring colors of nature: poppy, bluebell, rose and lilac. The sets will be available on the Live The Process e-commerce site, Net-a-Porter and Shopbop.
