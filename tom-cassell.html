Professional Life

Likewise, he started his YouTube channel, Syndicate, on 3 September 2010. However, he did not upload any videos until a year. He uploaded his first debut video titled “Syndicates Gaming Set Up – 150,000 Subscriber Video,” on May 6, 2011. The first video successfully garnered around 546,225 views.

Next, he uploaded another video titled “The Secret “8th Perk” ! Black Ops Zombies: Call Of The Dead (Read Description),” on the same day. This video went viral and has more than 2 million views.

Advertising violations

In late 2013, Tom had to pay $30,000, along with other influential YouTubers, to increase Microsoft’s newly launched Xbox One console as part of a promotional campaign managed by Microsoft’s advertising agency and run by Machinima Inc. The endorsement operated afoul of Federal Trade Commission (FTC) regulations due to “influencers”, including him, having failed to disclose that they “were paid by Microsoft to say nice things about Xbox One and its games”.

Gamasutra reported that he had again violated FTC guidelines on disclosures for YouTubers in August 2015, due to publishing many Let’s Play videos of 3BlackDot’s game Dead Realm without disclosing their financial ties to the product.

Further, he even got heavily criticized and became a defendant in a class-action lawsuit in July 2016 as he promoted the gambling website CSGOLotto.com.  It was against violating FTC regulations. He promised in a statement posted on Twitter that he would be more transparent in the future after his role at the company was revealed by others.
