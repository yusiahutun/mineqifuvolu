Margot Robbie Oozes Glamour in a Red Lace-Up Corset Minidress

May 2024 · 2 minute read

Margot Robbie’s streak of glamorous Barbie press-tour looks isn’t over yet.

Hours after appearing at the movie’s European premiere in a custom Vivienne Westwood gown complete with the brand’s signature corseted bodice, the star showed up at a VIP photo call in another jaw-dropping corseted dress.

This time, she and stylist Andrew Mukamal tapped designer Dilara Findikoglu for the pink-carpet ensemble. Eschewing the pink palette that has been typical of Robbie’s press-tour wardrobe so far, the actor instead wowed in a scarlet strapless minidress with a sculpted corset, a figure-hugging bodycon silhouette, and a lace-up detail down the back.

She finished off the fabulous outfit with yet more red-hot accessories, including glossy cherry-red heels and a matching red satin purse. While she wore no necklace, Robbie did rep a pair of pearl stud earrings.

While promoting Greta Gerwig’s highly anticipated film over the past month, Robbie and Mukamal have worked together to recreate an array of iconic looks worn by different iterations of the popular Mattel doll.

For instance, she made an appearance last month at Australia’s Bondi Beach wearing a black-and-white minidress reminiscent of the original 1959 doll’s black-and-white swimsuit. Since, she has also transformed into other Barbies over the decades, including a 1985 Barbie wearing a two-in-one power suit, a 1992 Barbie coordinating a silver belly chain with her silver drop earrings, and a 1960 Barbie sparkling in a black sequined evening gown.
