The Challenge veteran Nelson Thomas to have foot amputated... one year after serious car accident

May 2024 Â· 6 minute read

Nelson Thomas revealed he is having his foot amputated following the injuries he sustained in a fiery car accident in Austin, Texas on March 5, 2023.

On Sunday, the reality star, best known for appearing on The Challenge, shared the news that he has scheduled the surgery on the one-year anniversary of his wreck

'March 5th 2023 remains vivid in my memory, a persistent presence in my thoughts. I consciously embrace it as the initiation of a new chapter. Opting for amputation, I am determined to own March 5th and redefine its significance in my life,' the 35-year-old Inglewood native captioned the post. 

He concluded: 'Get ready, amputation world! I'm on my way! I am scheduled for surgery on March 5th, 2024.'

Alongside the video, Thomas shared various photos through his recovery, including gruesome images from the accident and injuries. 

The footage was set to audio of a conversation between him and his doctor coming to the conclusion that amputation is his best option. 

'I think we've attacked this as long as we've could,' he said, explaining he wants to move forward with his healing journey. 

Thomas said he had been asking himself about his quality of life and speaking with amputees to 'move on' with his life. 

In October, Thomas was charged with a DWI, seven months after surviving the horrific accident that caused his serious leg injury. 

Officers claimed Nelson had 'bloodshot, glassy eyes' as he described how another car had swerved in front of his own just before the accident, according to TMZ.

Thomas claimed he had consumed '2-3 mixed alcoholic drinks', but officers alleged The Challenge star had a 'strong odor of an alcoholic beverage emitting from his breath' during subsequent treatment at the hospital.

The results of a blood test Thomas underwent at the hospital alleged he had a BAC of .178, according to Page Six. This is more than double the legal limit of 0.08. His results elevated his charge to a Class A misdemeanor, TMZ reports.
