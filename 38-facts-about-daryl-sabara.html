Early Life and Career

Daryl Sabara, born on June 14, 1992, in Torrance, California, is an American actor. He began his acting career at a young age, appearing in various television shows and commercials.

Popular Role in Spy Kids

Sabara gained international recognition for his role as Juni Cortez in the hit movie series, “Spy Kids.” He portrayed the adventurous and tech-savvy secret agent with great skill and charm.

Voice Acting in Animation

In addition to his on-screen performances, Sabara has also lent his voice to several animated projects. He voiced the character of Hero Boy in the animated film “The Polar Express.”

Appeared in Disney Channel Series

Sabara had a recurring role in the Disney Channel series “Wizards of Waverly Place.” He played the character of T.J. Taylor, a troublemaking student at the Waverly Sub Station.

Collaboration with Robert Rodriguez

Sabara has had a long-standing collaboration with filmmaker Robert Rodriguez. He has appeared in various Rodriguez-directed projects, including the “Spy Kids” franchise and “Machete.”

Work in Theater

Besides his work in film and television, Sabara has also showcased his talents on stage. He performed in the musical “The Little Mermaid” at the Hollywood Bowl, playing the role of Prince Eric.

Music Video Appearances

Sabara has made appearances in numerous music videos, adding his unique presence to the visuals of various artists. One notable appearance was in the music video for Meghan Trainor’s hit song “Dear Future Husband.”

Relationship with Meghan Trainor

Speaking of Meghan Trainor, Sabara is married to the Grammy-winning singer-songwriter. The couple tied the knot on December 22, 2018, and often share their love and adventures on social media.

Guest Appearances on Television

Sabara has made guest appearances on popular television shows such as “Friends,” “Weeds,” and “Ultimate Spider-Man.” His versatile acting skills have allowed him to take on a variety of roles.

Contender on “Dancing with the Stars”

In 2018, Sabara participated in the celebrity dance competition show “Dancing with the Stars.” He showcased his dancing abilities and entertained the audience with his performances.

Philanthropic Efforts

Love for Animals

Passion for Photography

Recognition for his Talent
