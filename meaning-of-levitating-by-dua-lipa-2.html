Meaning of Levitating by Dua Lipa

The overall theme of Dua Lipa's song "Levitating" is a journey to a higher plane of existence through love and music. It explores the concept of transcending the ordinary and finding a sense of blissful escape in a world where music and connection create a realm of levitation.One standout lyric in the song is "If you wanna run away with me, I know a galaxy and I can take you for a ride." This lyric introduces the idea of leaving behind the mundane and entering a realm beyond the ordinary. It suggests that love and music can transport us to a different dimension, where possibilities are endless and joy is abundant.Another important lyric is "Glitter in the sky, glitter in my eyes, shining just the way I like." This lyric symbolizes the beauty and magic that exists in this otherworldly realm. It represents the spark and enchantment that love and music can bring into our lives. The imagery of glitter suggests a universe filled with celestial wonders, evoking a sense of awe and wonderment.The line "My love is like a rocket, watch it blast off" reveals a theme of passion and intensity. It portrays love as a force that propels us forward, taking us on an exhilarating journey. The word "rocket" also suggests an element of risk and adventure, highlighting the excitement and unpredictability of love.One of the most captivating lyrics is "You met me at the perfect time." This line suggests that love and connection have a way of finding us when we least expect it, guiding us towards a higher state of being. It implies that the timing of love is divine and fortuitous, aligning with the universe's grand plan.A more imaginative theme that can be derived from the lyrics is the power of collective energy. The song's repeated references to "we" and "us" imply that the experience of levitating is not solitary, but shared. It suggests that when individuals come together, their collective energy can create something extraordinary and elevate the human experience.Furthermore, the line "We can go wherever, so let's do it now or never" encourages embracing spontaneity and the present moment. This theme highlights the importance of seizing opportunities and living life to the fullest. It reminds us that in the realm of levitation, there are no limits or boundaries, and time is of the essence.An unexpected theme suggested by the lyrics is the idea of interconnectedness. The mention of being "written in the stars" and feeling forever connected implies a deeper bond that stretches beyond earthly limitations. It suggests that, in this elevated realm, love and music transcend physical boundaries and create bonds that transcend time and space.Lastly, the lyric "The milky way, we're renegading" evokes a sense of rebellion and breaking free from societal norms. It implies that in this realm of levitation, individuals can redefine conventional expectations and create their own path. It encourages embracing one's true self and celebrating the freedom that comes with defying expectations.In conclusion, "Levitating" by Dua Lipa is a captivating song that explores themes of transcendence, shared energy, seizing the moment, interconnectedness, and breaking free from societal constraints. Through its imaginative and unexpected lyrics, it invites listeners to escape from the ordinary and discover a higher plane of existence through love and music.
