Interpol Announces Album, Tour

May 2024 · 2 minute read

NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) Interpol has announced it has a new album, Marauder, coming out Aug. 24 and will support it with a tour.

The new single, “The Rover” is already out.

The news of the album/tour arrived via a press conference a news conference in Mexico City, according to Brooklyn Vegan, which has its own presale for the tour starting June 14 at 10 a.m.

Venues include the Hollywood Bowl with The Kills and Sunflower Bean and Madison Square Garden with Car Seat Headrest and Snail Mail in the band’s hometown of New York.

The trio is also set to play Brooklyn’s House of Van in August and Chicago’s Riot Fest in September.
