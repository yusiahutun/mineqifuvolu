How to link M-Pesa with Binance?

Before logging in to your Binance account, you must complete level 2 identity verification. This process ensures a high level of security for your money. If you are a first-time Binance user, here is the process for linking M-Pesa with Binance.

How to buy Bitcoin with M-Pesa on Binance?

Do you want to buy Bitcoin using your M-Pesa account but don't know how? Here are the simple steps to buy Bitcoin directly with M-Pesa.

  • Open the Binance application and click on “P2P Trading.”
  • Click on “Filter” to specify the payment method you want to use.
  • Choose the "M-Pesa" payment method and KES currency to buy crypto the tap "Confirm."

What is the minimum withdrawal from Binance?

According to Binance's official website, the minimum withdrawal crypto amount varies depending on the cryptocurrency you’re withdrawing. For example, the minimum withdrawal limit for Ethereum is 0.004 ETH, while the minimum for Bitcoin is 0.002 BTC.

When it comes to fiat currencies (such as USD or EUR), there’s a minimum withdrawal limit for each currency. For example, the minimum Euro amount you can withdraw is 10.00 EUR, while the minimum US Dollar amount is 50.00 USD.
