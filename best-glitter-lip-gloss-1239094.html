    How to Pick the Best Lip Gloss with Glitter?

    • Ingredients and Formula: Glitter lip glosses are meant to be emollients that nourish, hydrate, and moisturize the lips, without drying them after a point of time or getting sticky. To reap these benefits along with a glistening effect, look for ingredients like vitamins A and E, aloe vera, shea butter, coconut oil, beeswax, etc. Certain essential oils like grape or raspberry oils are also known to have lip-loving properties. To avoid chappy lips and a clumpy application, make sure your lip gloss has a medium to thin consistency. 
    • Finish: When it comes to gloss, it goes without saying that you are looking for a juicy, glistening coating on your lips. However, even lip glosses have varied finishes, for instance, a clear gloss gives off the effect of a lip oil, whereas a shimmery gloss will offer a glittery finish. The intensity of the color pigments differs too. Some picks may look quite dramatic while others may only enhance your natural lip tone. Keeping these factors in mind, choose your favorite from pearly, metallic, glittery, holographic, and chromatic finishes. 

    Pro tip: Some lip glosses offer a plumping effect and may have ingredients that are unfavorable for sensitive skin. Go for the ones suitable for all skin types and hypoallergenic. 

    • Staying power: It is worth noting that a lip gloss does not create a lasting effect as powerful as a matte or creamy lipstick. However, to avoid multiple re-touches, smudging, bleeding, and staining, make sure your lip gloss stays put for at least four to five hours. Check the label and reviews to make sure the formula is suitable for your needs. 

    Check out a few hacks that will help you optimize the use of your fresh-off-the-rack glossy lippies. 

    What is the Best Way of Applying Sparkly Lip Gloss?

    • Wearing on bare lips: The classic and most basic way of wearing your lipgloss is simply gliding it over your naked lips. You may use a clear gloss or a tinted one to achieve a juicy pout. Use the wand and start over from your cupid’s bow, extending to the corners and similarly glaze your lower lip.
    • Layering: The lip gloss also acts as a finisher step in the makeup, as you can first paint your lips with the desired lipstick — matte or creamy. Follow it up with a coating of the lustrous gloss to create a bold-shimmery look. 
    • DIY tinted gloss: For this trick, all you need is a clear lip gloss. Mix any of your lipsticks with the gloss and apply to the lips. This will allow you to create a new color lip gloss for every makeup look.

    FAQ's

    Does glitter lip gloss make lips look fuller?

    With a gentle overlining, you can make your lips appear fuller. Certain lip glosses have a plumping effect which can be used for fuller lips as well.

    Are glitter lip glosses safe?

    Yes, high-quality lip products from well-known brands are safe to use. If you have sensitive skin, check the ingredients label.

    Which brand of lip gloss is best?

    Bobbi Brown, NYX Professional, and e.l.f are certain brands that offer the best lip glosses.

    What glitter is used in lip gloss?

    Mica powder and cosmetic grade sparkle are used in lip glosses.

    What’s the difference between cosmetic glitter and craft glitter?

    The main difference is that cosmetic glitters are made of non-toxic acrylic components and certain plastic materials. On the other hand, craft glitter has metals and glass elements which are hazardous.

    Is it OK to put glitter on your lips?

    Yes, the glitter that is of a cosmetic grade and used in good quality lip glosses is okay to use on a daily basis

    How do I remove glitter lip gloss?

    You can use any makeup remover to perfectly clean your lips. Micellar water, remover oil, or wipes can be used.
