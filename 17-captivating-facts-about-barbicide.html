FAQs

1. What is Barbicide used for?

Barbicide is primarily used as a disinfectant solution to kill germs and bacteria on tools and surfaces in barber shops, hair salons, and spas. It helps maintain a clean and hygienic environment.

2. Can Barbicide be used on all types of surfaces?

Barbicide is safe to use on most surfaces, including stainless steel, plastic, glass, and ceramic. However, it is recommended to follow the instructions provided by the manufacturer and conduct a small patch test on a hidden area before using it on a new surface.

3. How long does it take for Barbicide to disinfect?

Barbicide takes approximately 10 minutes to effectively kill most germs and bacteria. It is important to let the tools or surfaces soak for the recommended time to ensure proper disinfection.

4. Is Barbicide safe to use around children and pets?

Barbicide is toxic if ingested and should be kept out of reach of children and pets. It is meant for professional use and should be handled with care. After disinfection, ensure thorough rinsing of tools to remove any residue before using them around children or pets.

5. Can Barbicide be used as a hand sanitizer?

No, Barbicide is not intended to be used as a hand sanitizer. It is formulated specifically for disinfecting tools, equipment, and surfaces in professional settings. For personal use, it is recommended to follow the guidelines provided by health authorities for hand sanitization.
