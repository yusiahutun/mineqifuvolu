The Blues Foundation Announces 2024 Blues Music Awards Winners

May 2024 · 4 minute read

MEMPHIS (CelebrityAccess) – The Blues Foundation proudly presented the 45th Annual Blues Music Awards on May 9 in Memphis, showcasing the pinnacle of talent and artistry within the blues genre. Among the distinguished honorees, guitarist Christone “Kingfish” Ingram emerged as a standout, clinching four impressive awards, including the coveted Album of the Year for Live In London. This accolade underscores his virtuosity and his significant contribution to the blues landscape.

In addition to Ingram’s remarkable achievements, notable figures such as Mike Zito, Bobby Rush, Ruthie Foster, and John Primer were celebrated for their outstanding contributions to blues music. Their collective triumphs, spanning multiple categories, underscore the depth and diversity of talent within the blues community, reaffirming its enduring relevance and artistic vibrancy.
