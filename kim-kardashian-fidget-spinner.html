Kim Kardashian Gold Fidget Spinner

April 2024 · 2 minute read

In case you've somehow been lucky enough to miss it, fidget spinners are the latest craze taking over. The hand-held toys, which claim to be "stress-relievers," have been carried by kids all over, along with everyone from Barron Trump to Kendall Jenner. And now, Kim Kardashian is getting in on the trend with a questionable fidget spinner all of her own.

In honor of Father's Day, Kardashian debuted a new drop of Kimoji merch over the weekend called "The Daddy's Girl" collection. The new merch includes a range of t-shirts, dad hats and more embroidered with not-at-all-creepy phrases like "Daddy," "Daddy's Girl," and of course, "Zaddy" because it's 2017.

Standing out as the star item, however, is the gold dollar sign fidget spinner engraved with "daddy," and aptly called the "Daddy Money Fidget Spinner."

Finally, a truly thoughtful gift for your father.

Kimoji Daddy Money fidget spinner, $15, kimoji.com.
