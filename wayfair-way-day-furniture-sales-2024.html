The Best Wayfair Way Day Furniture Sales in 2024

July 2024 · 3 minute read

Investing in high-quality furniture isn't always easy on the wallet, so if you’re looking for some new pieces, Wayfair's Way Day sale is a great way to score incredible savings.

The retailer's biggest sale of the year starts on May 4, and it will include up to 80 percent off everything from summer-ready outdoor furniture to comfortable sectionals that guests can fall asleep on.

Ahead, find all the sale details you need to know about, plus our favorite Way Day furniture deals.

What is Wayfair's Way Day sale?

Way Day is Wayfair's bi-annual blowout event that takes place in the spring and in the fall. Customers can enjoy up to 80 percent off all kinds of home categories, including top-rated bedding, home decor, and luxury furniture. Whether you need a gorgeous bed frame, comfortable accent chair, or durable outdoor sofa for your patio, you can find them all at huge discounts during Way Day.

Wayfair's first Way Day launched in 2018 and typically lasts two days.

When is Way Day 2024?

Wayfair's Way Day sale runs from May 4 to May 6.

In 2023, Wayfair's second Way Day took place on October 25 and 26. Expect to mark your calendar with similar dates this autumn.

What deals can I expect during Way Day?

Way Day includes some of the retailer's lowest prices of the year. Typically, discounts reach up to 80 percent, even on big-ticket items like bed frames and mattresses. This year, Wayfair is also offering free shipping on all orders.

Though you can shop amazing Way Day deals on Wayfair's site, the best ones will actually be available through Wayfair's mobile app. App users can score an extra 20 percent off their order when they use code WAYDAY20 at checkout.
