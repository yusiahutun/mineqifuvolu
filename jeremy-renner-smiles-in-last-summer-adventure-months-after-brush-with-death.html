Jeremy Renner smiles in 'last summer adventure' months after brush with death

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Jeremy Renner is back from his ‘last summer adventure’ and fans are glad to see the Marvel actor back to enjoying life with family after a near brush with death this January.

Jeremy Renner has been active on social media ever since his accident, expressing gratitude and sharing snippets of him spending time with his loved ones. The movie star has also been active in volunteering for policymaking in home state Nevada.

Jeremy Renner smiles in ‘last summer adventure’

Celebrity Jeremy Renner has posted snaps from his ‘last summer adventure’ with his daughter.

He posted an Instagram photo of himself with the caption, “Posting up to bid you an amazing weekend… please find time to share time with those you love and that love you back … it all that really matters anyway.”

Renner’s post has also touched hearts of his followers who are commending his courage of bouncing back to the reality and taking the time out to spend with his loved ones.

    • Renner had a near brush of death only months back

    Jeremy Renner had an almost fatal snowplow accident on New Year’s Day when he was out in the snow with his nephew.

    The accident caused 30+ broken bones as well as orthopedic injuries and blunt chest trauma.

    The Hollywood star even remembered typing a note on his phone for his family members instructing them, “Don’t let me live in tubes on a machine and if my existence is going to be on drugs and painkillers, just let me go now.”

    Renner’s accident happened due to the snow plough machine losing its control and running over him causing critically injuries. The Hawkeye actor later revealed, “I was awake through all of it, it’s exactly how you’d imagine it feels like… I was on asphalt and ice… it feels like someone took the wind out of you. Too many things are going on in the body to feel pain, it’s everything. It’s like if your soul could have pain.”

    Fans celebrate Renner’s recovery

    Renner’s Instagram followers are celebrating the fast recovery of the TV actor. One emotional fan commented: “I’m so glad you got your life back and that you had the gumption to put in the work effort and time to make it happen. Blessings are free we just have to accept them.”

    “Surrounding yourself with the people you love is the best. Also, you look amazing,” a second comment read.

    “We are all so proud of you! Thank you for all you do!,” said another fan!
