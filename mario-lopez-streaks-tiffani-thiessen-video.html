If you got it, flaunt it. Thankfully, these are words Mario Lopez lives by.

Almost 25 years since the premiere of "Saved by the Bell," the guy formerly known as A.C. Slater is still showing off his shredded physique.

Mario stripped to his Rated M skivvies yesterday at The Grove after losing a Super Bowl bet to his "EXTRA" cohost Maria Menounos. Remember when Maria had to host the show in Times Square last year in a bikini? Talk about half-naked revenge!

And one of his Bayside High alums is still impressed.

The gorgeous Tiffani Thiessen (who will always be Kelly Kapowski to us) talks about Lopez's stunt today on "Anderson Live" -- saying "he's been doing it since he was 15."

"He definitely didn't have a problem taking his shirt off," she says of their days together on the classic show. "He looked like that then and he looks the same now."

"I guess I would [strip] too, if I looked like that," she added.

Kelly Tiffani needs to give herself some credit though -- we've seen recent photos of her in her underwear for Me In My Place and she still looks incredible too!
