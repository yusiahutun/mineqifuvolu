Ocean's 8 comes to London! Rihanna, Sandra Bullock &amp; Cate Blanchett shimmer on the red carpet

April 2024 · 2 minute read

It was full on glamour in London's Leicester Square on Wednesday evening as the A-list gathered for the premiere of much-anticipated movie Ocean's 8. The film's stars wore some gorgeous outfits for the event, with Rihanna, Sandra Bullock, Cate Blanchett and Helena Bonham Carter wowing in very different outfits on the red carpet. Rihanna wore a show stopping golden gown with plunging neckline and matching gold sandals, while Sandra chose the most divine sequin striped jumpsuit by Zuhair Murad, which retails for a pricy £6354. Cate was stunning as ever in a chic grey feathered top with a diamond and emerald earring we can't stop staring at!

Rihanna has everyone talking with her crinkle-effect gown which fell off the shoulders for a sassy look. The singer accessorised with two pairs of gold hoop earrings by Konstantino Jewllery's Flamenco Gold Collection. The star wore her hair in a cool high bun and finished her look with a bright red lip colour.

Sandra and Helena looked super glam as they posed together for photographers. Helena's floor-length silver dress is by the Vivienne Westwood Couture Collection and she wore her hair in her signature wild look. Sandra teamed her amazing multi coloured jumpsuit with some simple black sandals and styled her hair in a natural down look.

Sarah Paulson wore an eye-catching salmon pink dress for the premiere which fell in tiers over her figure. The actress wore coordinating pink drop earrings to complete the look and her hair in a slicked back style.

Ocean's 8 sees eight women plan and execute a heist at New York City's star-studded annual Met Gala. The film follows Debbie Ocean, played by Oscar winner Sandra, as she tries to assemble the perfect crew anchored by Cate's character Lou. The perfect movie for a girls' night out, we say.

Ocean's Eight opens in UK cinemas on 22 June.
