Smart thermostats are incredibly convenient, allowing people to automate their home's heating and cooling system.

The ability to connect your home's thermostat to a Wi-Fi connection means you can control your smart thermostat from anywhere, even if you aren't at home. This not only allows you to schedule the right temperature for your environment but also helps you save costs on your energy bill. It's the perfect way to start building a smart home as many smart thermostats offer integration with other products like locks, cameras, and more.

When looking for the best smart thermostats, it's important to understand whether you need to purchase a smart thermostat with or without a C wire.

What Is a C Wire?

The C wire (common wire) powers a smart thermostat by providing a constant flow of 24 VAC (volts AC).

The term "constant flow" is the key here, because the C wire isn't the source of power. The R wires are the actual source of power, but they are not continuous, hence the use of the C wire.

However, not all home wiring consists of a C wire. The good news is, some of the best smart thermostats do not require the power from the C wire to work.

In fact, some of the best smart thermostats like the Google Nest Thermostat have no requirement for the C wire as they contain rechargeable batteries or other technology that removes the need for one. Here's a look at some of the best.

1. Google Nest Thermostat

Google's Nest Thermostat is a simple smart thermostat that uses a rechargeable lithium-ion battery. This bypasses the need for the C wire, offering an always-on display that details the current temperature setting.

Compatible with most voice assistants like Google Assistant and Amazon Alexa, the Google Nest Thermostat can be adjusted easily using voice commands, or via the handy smartphone app.

From one simple dashboard, you can see how much energy you've saved, invite other family members to control the smart thermostat, and set temperature schedules in each of your rooms.

Best of all, the Google Nest Thermostat is incredibly easy to install and is one of the most versatile Wi-Fi thermostats with no C wire.
