Too Hot To Handles Cam and Emily wish they'd broken rules for secret first night romp

April 2024 · 3 minute read

When Too Hot to Handle first featured on our screens last year, fans quickly fawned over the cast and premise of the show.

Fresh singles head into a villa in a bid to find romance - but no kissing and no acts of a sexual nature are allowed whatsoever.

Contestants from all over the world go in with a $100,000 prize fund up for grabs, but any rule breaking sees money deducted from the winner - and only one person will be able to get the cash this year.

The biggest rule breakers? Emily Miller and Cam Holmes, who couldn't keep their hands off each other from their very first interaction.

With kisses, grinding and "crab grabs", the money pot quickly emptied - to the point where the pair think they should have just jumped each others' bones to begin with.

Speaking to Daily Star Online, Cam and Emily recalled their time on the abstinence retreat, Cameron said: "We may as well have just had sex because we spent more than that on little weird loopholes.

"We should have had sex on the first day and got it over and done with!"

However, for the sake of Lana - the all-knowing device which dishes out punishments and money deductions - the pair avoided sex, and were proud of themselves when they came back from a private night thinking they had won back $10,000 for not breaking the rules... except they did.

Cameron said: "When we went into the private suite, I thought it wasn’t going to go well but we woke up in the morning and I thought wow we’ve done it.

"We were so convinced that we had done it and nothing had happened and that’s why we walked in and we were celebrating and told everyone we won the money back."

Lana soon broke the news that they hadn't successfully completed the challenge - but Emily was shocked to hear that, adding: "We didn't even kiss! I thought we had behaved so well but I guess Lana is a bit picky."

The pair defiantly said that they didn't realise that dry humping was a rule break - which cost them $10,000 for the night.

Despite the sexual acts they did together costing them the cash, Cam took it upon himself to "make an investment" by giving some self-gratification.

"The financial loss was not worth all the rule breaks," Emily said. "I can’t believe Cam went and did some self gratification! It was not worth it at all."

"For me it was justified, I needed it - it was an investment! It would’ve been worse if I hadn’t done it," Cam quipped.

Emily also slammed the $4,000 loss over a "crab grab" adding: "It was pathetic! It wasn't worth it. I wish we'd never done it."

Despite losing a chunk of cash between them, Cam admits he doesn't regret losing money for kissing Emily.

He added: "Weirdly the kisses were the thing that was most worth it for me. The other stuff was sexual silly stuff but kisses helped us form a connection with each other.

"Not having sex was very difficult but not kissing was so challenging because it’s something you go to do so naturally."

Too Hot to Handle is available to stream now on Netflix.
