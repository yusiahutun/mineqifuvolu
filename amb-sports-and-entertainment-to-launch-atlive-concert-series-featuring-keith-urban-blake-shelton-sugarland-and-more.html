AMB Sports and Entertainment To Launch ATLive Concert Series Featuring Keith Urban, Blake Shelton, S

March 2024 · 2 minute read

ATLANTA (CelebrityAccess) – AMB Sports and Entertainment (AMBSE) has announced the launch of ATLive, Mercedes-Benz Stadium’s inaugural concert series on Friday, November 15 and Sunday, November 17, 2019.

Friday’s line-up will feature performances by newly crowned 2018 CMA and Academy of Country Music’s (ACM) Entertainer of the Year and four-time GRAMMY Award winner Keith Urban; Country Music Association’s (CMA) 2012 Entertainer of the Year, five-time Male Vocalist of the Year and GRAMMY nominee Blake Shelton; AMA’s 2015 New Artist of the Year and GRAMMY-nominated Sam Hunt; platinum-selling and GRAMMY Award-winning Sugarland; 2018’s Best New Alternative Rock Artist at the iHeart Radio Music Awards, Judah & the Lion; and singer-songwriter Cale Dodds.

Sunday’s line-up will feature three-time CMA and six-time ACM Award winner Eric Church; multi-platinum, ACM, CMA, Country Music Television (CMT) and Billboard Music Award-winning singer-songwriter Luke Combs; four-time CMA and five-time ACM winner and GRAMMY-nominated Brothers Osborne; and soulful newcomer, Caylee Hammack.

“We are very excited to bring such a talented group of artists together for an incredible music experience,” said Tim Zulawski, senior vice-president and chief revenue officer, AMB Sports and Entertainment. “From the beginning, our goal was to create a concert series that will transcend what most expect and become the must-see event for Atlanta. And in doing so, allows us the opportunity to give back to our communities and those in need.  We are committed to delivering world-class experiences and ATLive is just the next opportunity to do it.”

Tickets for each night are scheduled to go on-sale Friday, August 23 at 10am ET.

AMBSE in partnership with the Arthur M. Blank Family Foundation will be donating a portion of the proceeds from ATLive to two organizations, the Johnnie Mac Soldier’s Fund and the Quest Community Development Organization.
