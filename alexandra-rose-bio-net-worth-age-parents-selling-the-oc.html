Meet Alexandra Rose! Bio, Net Worth, Age, Dating

March 2024 · 5 minute read

Alexandra Rose is one of the star agents at The Oppenheim Group who is starring in Netflix’s Selling The OC. This article covers details on her parents, siblings, age, net worth, and relationship.

Keep scrolling down to learn more about Alexandra Rose.

Alexandra Rose On Netflix’s Selling The OC

On August 24, 2022, “Selling the OC,” a spin-off to Selling Sunsets, had its Netflix debut. In Orange County, California, the show follows eleven brand-new real estate brokers as they market opulent waterfront residences.

“Selling Sunset” stars The Oppenheim Group, a brokerage run by Jason and Brett Oppenheim, will reportedly branch out to Newport Beach in 2021, according to the Real Deal. They brought a brand-new cast of agents along with them. Alexandra is also one of the 11 agents who will be on the show.

Speaking with Today, Alexandra uttered that the three words describing her this season are “Vulnerable, hungry, and ruthless”. She also made friends with Alexandra Jarvis who shared, “Rose and I are the top producing female agents in the office”. They labeled each other as “unstoppable”.

Two Alexandras met each other in a professional setting but they became good friends.

In a recent exclusive interview with ET, Rose and Jarvis essentially revealed their interpersonal bond, as well as their group standing, is the same as it was during filming. “I don’t talk to [most of the realtors at the brokerage],” Rose said. “I just address the issues and address the rumors that they’re stirring. And they get offended because I’m confronting them about what they’re talking about and their gossip… They think I’m just stirring the pot and starting drama. It’s just such bulls**t.”

Jarvis added to that statement, “I would second that because it’s like if they’re trying to say [Rose is] the pot stirrer, I mean, it’s like Alex Hall made the pot of soup and then gave her the spoon.”

Others joining her in this 8-episode journey are Alex Hall, Alexandra Jarvis, Austin Victoria, Brandi Marshall, Gio Helou, Kayla Cardona, Lauren Brito, Polly Brindle, Sean Palmieri, and Tyler Stanaland.

Alexandra Rose Net Worth

At the time of the premiere of the show, Alexandra Rose’s net worth measures $1 million. According to her LinkedIn, she started her career at Nordstrom in sales where she worked from August 2013 to March 2018. From 2019 to 2021, she worked at Sotheby’s International Realty in real estate sales.

Starting from February 2021, Alexandra is currently working at The Oppenheim Group as Real Estate Agent.

According to her company’s bio, Alexandra made close to $100M in sales in her first 4 years as a realtor. She brings a solid understanding of the dynamic real estate market, allowing her to assist her clients in making smart decisions that align with their real estate goals. Her professional background in sales and client service ensures her clients receive personalized guidance and can rest assured that her expertise, knowledge, and patience will make home buying and selling an enjoyable experience.

Furthermore, she earned a reputation for her integrity, professionalism, and tireless work ethic, Alexandra Rose brings local market knowledge, exceptional negotiation skills, and superior client service to every transaction. She prides herself on the ability to meticulously handle a multitude of tasks with a positive, can-do attitude — always putting her clients first.

Alexandra holds Bachelor’s Degree in Finance and Economics from Grand Canyon University.

Before her gig in the corporate world, Alexandra pursued a career as an actress. Alexandra performed in theatre while in Arlington, Texas in the productions of Agamemnon, Hollywood Showcase, Isolation, and Backstage Pass to Comedy Alley. She also starred in two short films namely Entertainment Tonight and Calypso.

Alexandra trained at Dennis Lavalle Actors Workshop, KD Studio, HighPoint Prep, Tarrant County College, Advantage Academy, and Texas Actors Seminar.

Who Are Alexandra Rose Parents?
