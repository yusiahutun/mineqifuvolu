Sydney Sierota’s Bio, Wiki

Sydney Sierota was born as Sydney Grace Ann Sierota on 21st April 1997 in Los Angeles, California, Sydney Sierota is a music artist. As per her birth date, her sun sign is Taurus which defines her personality to be positive, practical, and ambitious. And she is 22 years old as of now. Furthermore, her nationality is American and as far as her ethnicity is concerned, she is White.

Moreover, Sierota was born in a musical family as her father is a songwriter and her three siblings, two brothers Noah Sierota, Jamie Sierota, and Graham Sierota are all musicians. When she was very young, she started singing at the churches and later joined her sibling’s band, ”Ready Set Go!” now ”Echosmith.” Further, she used to attended high school like normal kids but was homeschooled after the Echosmith‘s schedule became tight.

Likewise, Sierota became the lead singer of the band whereas her brothers Jamie sings & plays guitar, Noah plays bass & sings backup vocals, and Graham plays drums. Initially, they released several cover songs. Moreover, in 2012, they signed the recording contract with Warner Bros Records and released their debut album, Talking Dreams on 1st October 2012. The album hit #1 on US Heatseekers Albums (Billboard) and certified with gold by RIAA.

Following the success of the album, they released several hit singles. As of 2018, they are working on their next album whose name is still undisclosed.
