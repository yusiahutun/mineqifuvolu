How do you respond to a message on tinder?

Here are some general guidelines to keep in mind:

  • Be authentic: It's important to be yourself and not try to be someone you're not. This will make your conversation more genuine and enjoyable for both parties.
  • Start with a greeting: A simple "Hi" or "Hello" is a good way to start the conversation. If the other person has included something interesting in their profile, you can use that as a conversation starter.
  • Respond to the message: If the other person has asked you a question or made a comment, respond to it in your reply. This will show that you're interested in the conversation.
  • Ask a question: Asking a question is a good way to keep the conversation going. You can ask about the other person's interests or hobbies or ask for their opinion on a topic.
  • Be positive: Try to keep the conversation positive and avoid negative comments or complaints. This will make the conversation more enjoyable for both parties.
