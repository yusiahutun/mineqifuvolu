49 Best Date Night Ideas Near Me

June 2024 · 4 minute read
1

Hike a scenic trail.

Getty Images

Or try an even more adventurous outdoor activity together, like white water rafting, kayaking, paddle boarding, or surfing.

2

Visit an aquarium, zoo, or wildlife preserve.

Alexandra Dudkina / EyeEm//Getty Images

Visiting an aquarium isn't just fun, it's also good for your health, as watching fish move through the water can be relaxing and soothing for people. See other types of animals at your nearest zoo or head to a nearby wildlife preserve to observe birds and other animals in their natural habitat.

3

Camp in your backyard.

Getty Images

Set up a campfire, toast some marshmallows, and knock back some homemade mulled wine while you spend a romantic night outdoors.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

4

Go on your first date—again!

Getty Images

Take a walk down memory lane and re-create your very first date.

5

Be a tourist in your own town.

Klaus Vedfelt//Getty Images

If there are places in your local area you've been meaning to visit—or spots you like but haven't been to in a long time—make your date about exploring places in your own community. You'll be supporting local businesses, and you just might find your new favorite haunt.

6

Volunteer together.

Getty

Strengthen your bond and share your love with others by volunteering at an animal shelter, soup kitchen, or another local charity organization.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

7

Try a ropes course or zip-line.

WALTER ZERLA//Getty Images

For a high-flying adventure within nature, head to a local ropes course or zip-line park—it's especially fun in fall when the leaves are changing.

8

Take a mixology class.

Jordan Lye//Getty Images

Up your skills behind the bar by taking a class that'll teach you all about proper drink-making techniques and give you a few new ideas to try at home. You can even try an online class from the comfort of your home!

9

Head to a regional rodeo.

davidf//Getty Images

If you love having a "boot scootin good time" and horses, put on your coziest jeans and boots for a evening out with your beau at a local rodeo. Visit prorodeo.com and rodeosusa.com to find a competition in your area.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

10

Take a cooking class.

Getty Images

Spend an evening learning how to cook or bake a new dish together, or you can save money by taking a free "class" at home. There are several popular YouTube channels you can watch to learn how to make something scrumptious, or be your own teachers using a new recipe you find online.

11

Listen to a poetry reading.

georgeclerk//Getty Images

Many independent bookstores or coffee shops offer a range of readings by talented writers and poets in the area that will ensure an inspiring date night.

12

Sign up for a trail ride.

urbancow//Getty Images

Research reputable nearby stables and sign up for a guided trail ride, which work well for beginning riders.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

13

Visit a museum.

Tim Grist Photography//Getty Images

You can never go wrong with a trip to a museum, whether it's an art museum, science museum, or historical museum. Many museums also offer late-night events throughout the year, like the free Friday nights offered at New York City's Museum of Modern Art.

14

Take a dance class together.

SDI Productions//Getty Images

It's never too late to learn how to tango (or samba or salsa dance for that matter). Check with local community centers and dance studios to sign up for a couple's dance class.

15

Visit a local farm.

Getty

Pick whatever's in season—apples and pumpkins in the fall, strawberries in the spring, and cherries and blueberries during the summer.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

16

Go for sunset picnic.

Getty Images

Bring some wine, cheese, and fresh fruit to snack on while you take in the beautiful landscape.

17

Go stargazing.

Getty Images

Bring a thermos filled with your favorite drink and a few cozy blankets for a romantic night under the stars.

18

Pick out your favorite records together.

HEX//Getty Images

The best part about going record shopping together—besides finding some new vinyls to add to your collection—is sharing your favorite artists with each other.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

19

Make an elaborate brunch at home.

Getty Images20

Visit a vineyard.

Getty Images

Enjoy a wine tasting, then picnic among the rows of grapes, and finish the day with a romantic walk around the property.
