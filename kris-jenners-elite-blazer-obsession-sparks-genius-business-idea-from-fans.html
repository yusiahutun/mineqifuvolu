Kris Jenners elite blazer obsession sparks genius business idea from fans Sat 4 Novemb

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Kris Jenner is stunning her fans this November as she shares a video of herself wearing one of her trademark pieces of clothing. The momager dons a structured blue blazer as she mimes audio from her daughter, Kim Kardashian, in the clip.

The Kardashians star’s look is blowing people away as she rakes in compliments in the comments section. The 67-year-old has been wearing some striking blazers during season 4 and, by the looks of it, she’s showing no signs of swapping her look for something different any time soon.

Kris Jenner’s blue blazer

Promoting a brand on her Instagram page, Kris Jenner had some of her followers laughing as the family is often taking to social media with brand partnerships.

Aside from what she was actually selling on the ‘gram, more people were interested in Kris’ ageless look – specifically her blue blazer.

One fan asked: “Why is nobody talking about how good she looks?”

Another commented: “Mother is mothering.”

More said they loved to see Kris “in blue,” and another wrote: “All I know is I need this blue blazer.”

View Instagram Post

Kris has been pulling out the blazers

Mom-of-six Kris could be dubbed a ‘trailblazer of blazers’ at this point for all the times she’s been spotted wearing the tailored garment.

During the family’s fourth installment of their Hulu show, Kris has been donning blazers in all kinds of colors.

Episode 4 saw Scott Disick taken aback as Kris arrived at his house with Khloé Kardashian.

Kris racked up in a neon yellow pantsuit and Scott said: “Wow. That’s some color. This is such a cute outfit.”

He added: “I like suits on you…”

Khloé chimed in: “She always wears suits.”

Fans are convinced by business idea

After Kris debuted her neon number, she was wearing another cream blazer in a confessional.

The Kardashians’ next episode saw the Get Safely founder wearing another suit jacket at True Thompson‘s birthday party, so it’s safe to say she’s found her groove in the blazer department.

Kris’ penchant for the item hasn’t gone unnoticed by fans as many are suggesting she should bring out her own blazer line.

One Twitter user asked: “Why hasn’t Kris Jenner come out with her own line of blazers yet? Seems like a missed opportunity”

Another tweeted: “Kris Jenner needs to drop a blazer/suit jacket line in her near future.”

More simply wrote that the star’s blazers are “elite.”
