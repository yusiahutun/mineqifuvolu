WWE SmackDown is an American professional wrestling television program produced by WWE and created by Vince McMahon. Also called Friday Night SmackDown or just SmackDown, it’s one of WWE’s two flagship programs (the other one being Monday Night Raw). Similar to Raw, WWE wrestlers get in the ring to fight and entertain the crowd. The show first aired on April 29, 1999, and has since been broadcasted from 163 arenas, 148 cities and towns, and six countries (the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, Japan, Italy, and Mexico). WWE SmackDown is currently in its 26th season and has produced an impressive 1,286 episodes.
