Super Bowl: Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's parents' adorable moments

September 2024 · 3 minute read

During the Super Bowl last night (February 11), Taylor Swift was in attendance to support her boyfriend Travis Kelce, but she wasn’t alone as both their parents also stepped out at the game.

Taylor Swift and her NFL-playing beau went public in September when she began showing up to cheer him on. She and Travis Kelce have since been fairly open about their time together, whether it be PDA-filled kisses or Travis gushing about her in interviews and on the podcast he does with his brother Jason Kelce. But now the whole clan has been seen together supporting the 34-year-old during the biggest game of the season, and we can’t get enough of Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce’s parents getting along like a house on fire.

Taylor Swift and her family support Travis Kelce at the Super Bowl

Taylor Swift was seen in one of the VIP boxes at the Allegiant Stadium in Las Vegas for the Super Bowl with both her and Travis’ family. Both of her parents joined their daughter to cheer on Travis who, as part of the Kansas City Chiefs, won the 2024 Super Bowl against the San Francisco 49ers with a score of 25-22.

Pictures showed the celebrity couple’s two dads, Ed Kelce and Scott Swift, talking as they waited for the game to begin while Taylor was seen greeting and embracing Travis’ brother Jason, 36, and his wife Kylie.

Footage was then shared to X (formerly Twitter) of Taylor and her dad talking with Kylie and all three seemed to be in high spirits as they couldn’t stop smiling.

Travis’ mom Donna and his brother Jason were seen sitting behind Taylor, 34, throughout the game. The celebrity was seen frequently turning around during the match to talk to them both. Taylor’s mom Andrea sat in the same row as the music star and other famous faces, including Taylor’s collaborator and friend, rapper Ice Spice – who caused a stir with her upside-down cross.

71-year-old Donna wore a jacket that was custom-made by Stoney Clover and featured a heart with her son’s Chiefs number, 87, in the middle. Jason went with red and yellow Chief-colored overalls. The shirt he wore donned the nickname he gave his younger brother, Big Yeti. Kylie did something similar by wearing a shirt with her brother-in-law’s slogan, “Alright, Nah,” on it. Kylie is a Philadelphia Eagles fan and so has a rule she won’t wear Chiefs merch.

Taylor’s bond with Travis’ family is what he ‘loves’ about her

With the out pour of support for Travis throughout this season from both of their loved ones, it’s no surprise Taylor Swift has bonded very well with Travis’ family. Donna has even given her stamp of approval to their relationship by gifting Taylor a ring – how sweet!

A source revealed Tay Tay’s bond with his family is what he “loves most” about her.

The insider claims: “Everybody knows how close Travis is with his family. So it really means everything to him how seamlessly Taylor blends in with all of them. His family is all about just hanging out, joking, and having a great time.

“It’s almost like she’s known his family forever, and he loves that about her.”

They added that the Love Story songstress is “really down to earth” and is something Travis also appreciates about her, as it’s “exactly” how his family is too.
