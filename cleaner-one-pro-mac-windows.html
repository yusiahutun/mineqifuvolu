One of the Best PC and Mac Cleaners to Tune Up Your Device

June 2024 · 4 minute read

With better technology comes bigger prices. So, it's no wonder people are upgrading or replacing their computers less frequently than they once were. The problem, however, is that the more you store on computers, the more programs you install, and the longer you have them, the slower they tend to become.

But, if you maintain your computer well by regularly "cleaning" it, you could squeeze a lot more life out of it than previously thought. That's especially true with apps like Cleaner One Pro.

What Is Cleaner One Pro?

Developed by TrendMicro, Cleaner One Pro is software that can be used on Windows and Mac computers. It's free to download and helps your computer run faster by cleaning up unnecessary junk, files, folders, and more.

It can also identify any possible security risks that could hinder your computer, making it feel like you're using a brand-new machine every day.

By removing unwanted or unneeded files, you'll save valuable disk space. Not only does this stop your PC from reaching its storage limit, but it also saves you money by not having to invest in a new SSD or external hard drive.

Similarly, once files have been deleted, Cleaner One Pro can help you to clean them up, ensuring they don't clog up your operating system. And how many times have you re-downloaded the same app, photo, or similar? You guessed it, Cleaner One Pro can also help to identify duplicate files so you keep the ones that matter the most.

Cleaner One Pro’s Features

Whether you're running macOS or Windows, Cleaner One Pro offers a wealth of useful features that can help clean up and speed up your computer. Let's take a look at how this software can help you.

Cleaning

  • Smart Scan: Give your PC a customized check-up in a single click. Identify any potential issues or ways in which your PC's performance can be improved.
  • Junk Files: Check your PC for any unwanted or unneeded files and remove them in an instant to free up disk space.
  • Big Files: Identify large files on your computer that may no longer be required and save yourself some valuable storage.
  • Duplicate Files: Perhaps you've downloaded the same movie multiple times. Now you can get rid of duplicate files by cleaning them up with Cleaner One Pro.
  • Similar Photos: Do you have more than one similar photo? Are you struggling to decide which photos to keep? Select which ones are important and remove the rest.
  • Disk Map: Scan your computer to get a visual view of what files are taking up the most room. Using Disk Map, you can analyze everything on your computer and get rid of the junk you don't need.

Application Management

  • Startup Manager: If your computer is taking a long time to boot up, it might be time to identify what apps are loading upon startup. Most of these probably aren't important, so you can speed up your OS's boot time in a few clicks.
  • App Manager: Manage and organize your apps easily and uninstall unwanted apps. It's easy to download a few different apps to find the right one for your needs, but that doesn't mean you should keep the ones that are no good!

Privacy Protection

  • File Shredder (macOS): Do you have sensitive information and data kept on your Mac? Securely delete sensitive files so that they become impossible to recover should someone gain access or steal your device.

Optimization

  • Turbo Booster (Windows): Optimize your PC's performance so you can focus on the things that matter the most. Work on the files you need then play the games you want, uninterrupted by slow speeds.
  • Registry Cleaner (Windows): After deleting unnecessary files and apps, your PC's registry can become clogged up. By using the registry cleaner, you can eliminate any potential errors or crashes.

Should You Get Cleaner One Pro?

With so many helpful features that can help clean up and speed up your computer, Cleaner One Pro is a worthy investment. Plus, you don't need any technical knowledge to use it; save yourself time, stress, and money by optimizing your existing devices rather than purchasing new ones.

Cleaner One Pro is available for just $19.99 for one device on a 1-year plan. And, if you have any issues, TrendMicro's incredible tech support is on hand 24/7.
