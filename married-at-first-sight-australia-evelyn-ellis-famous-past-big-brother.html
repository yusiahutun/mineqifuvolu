Evelyn appeared on another major reality show

    Married at First Sight Australia star Evelyn Ellis' is one of the several contestants that has found fame thanks to series ten of the popular programme - but did you know this isn't her first reality TV stint?

    If you thought Evelyn looked familiar, that's because she has appeared on the small screen before. Read on to find out all about her famous past.

    Evelyn, who hails from Sydney, was a housemate on the 17th season of , which aired in 2016. The reality star was just 18 years old when she took part in the show. 

    The now-27-year-old lasted 50 days in the house and came in 5th place in the competition. Watch the moment fellow housemate Laura Carter confronted Evelyn after she complained about Alex Cannon, with whom she enjoyed a romance while in the house.

    Are Evelyn and Rupert still together?

    While things seemed to be going well between the couple, Evelyn decided to opt out of the relationship during the final vow ceremony. 

    Explaining her decision, Evelyn told Rupert: "It took you six weeks to take me on a date and that was only when I asked you. That says a lot.

    "Someone who sees my worth would have put his ego aside and fight for me as I fought for you. So, I choose not to continue this relationship."

    Rupert felt the same way, responding: "A platonic connection is not enough to sustain a healthy and loving relationship. I feel you didn't put your best foot forward to progress this relationship."
