Get To Know Carmela Raber Age, Net Worth, Return To Amish

October 2024 · 5 minute read

The relationship between Return to Amish star, Jeremiah Raber, and his wife, Carmela Raber has been full of ups and downs. The couple first met through social media and shortly after tied the wedding knot. Their wedding was well documented in the Season 3 finale of Return to Amish.

Find out if the couple were still together in 2023 by reading the wiki article below. Also, learn about Carmela Raber’s grandkids, maiden name, job, and more.

Return To Amish: Are Carmela Raber And Jeremiah Raber Still Together?

Jeremiah made his reality TV debut in 2012 when he appeared on Season 1 of Breaking Amish, which followed him and the other four costars as they move to New York City in order to experience a different life and decided if they want to remain part of the Amish community or remain outside them.

Shortly after the Breaking Amish’s premiere, Jeremiah split from Naomi Stutzman, his first wife, with whom he was married from 2005 to 2011. The pair share three children.

Jeremiah met Carmela, who herself has four kids from a previous relationship, through social media. In 2016, Carmela made her reality TV debut alongside him on the 3rd season of Return to Amish, Breaking Amish’s spinoff show. The 3rd season of the show concluded with Jeremiah and Carmela’s wedding.

However, within one year of their marriage, the couple made headlines when Jeremiah was arrested for domestic violence in response to the complaint filed by Carmela, alleging that her husband struck her on a daily basis. However, the charges against Jeremiah were dropped in July 2018 after he completed a pretrial diversion.

The turmoil in their relationship did not slow down as in October 2017 Jeremiah went on a Facebook rant against Carmel and accused her of stealing $15,000 from him. In response, Carmela accused Jeremiah of physically abusing her. “I’m so scared but I’m free from his manipulation and he can go ahead and spread his lies but in the end, [the] truth will come out,” Carmela wrote in a since-deleted Facebook post.

After fighting with each other in social media posts, the duo reconciled for the second time before splitting again in 2022. In March 2022, Carmela filed for a restraining order against her husband, alleging that her husband threatened to put her 6 feet under.

In response, Jeremiah claimed that all of Carmela’s allegations are baseless. Furthermore, in his interview with In Touch in March 2022, he said that all of her accusations were about things that happened in 2017 and it’s all in the reports from back then.

In May 2022, Jeremiah confirmed his split with Carmela after she filed a restraining order against him. He also took to Instagram to write, “Even though we’re not together, I will still love him,” sharing a video of the song “I Will Always Love You” performed by Whitney Houston.

Now, fast forward to 2023, and it looks like the two are now again together. Reply to a user’s comment asking if Carmela is now single, Jeremiah replied, “No, We’re still married and working things out.”

In March 2023, the Return to Amish Season 7 trailer revealed that the couple is trying to have a baby now.

Carmela Raber Grandkids

Carmela Raber has four children from a previous relationship and a grandkid.

Her children are Jordan, River Mendez, Malachi Gibson, and Savannah Mendez. Reportedly, Carmela had her first baby, Jordan, when she was only 16. Posting a picture with her daughter, Savannah, Carmela wished her child the happiest 7th birthday on Facebook.

In case you did not know, Carmela is also a grandmother to her granddaughter, Kehlanni. On June 9, 2018, she posted a picture with her granddaughter Kehlanni. Kehlanni is the daughter of Carmela’s first child, Jordan.

Carmela Raber Age

Born in 1981, Carmela Raber turned 41 years old in late 2022.

What Is Carmela Raber Maiden Name?

Carmela Raber’s maiden name is Mendez. On her Facebook, Carmela shared that the people with “Mendez” as their maiden name are famous for their beautiful face. As per the post, they are also kind, resilient, and strong.

Unfortunately, Carmela lost her mother on March 30, 2020. Remembering her, Carmela posted the picture of her mom to her Facebook on July 16, 2021.
