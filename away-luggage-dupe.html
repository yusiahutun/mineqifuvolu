This Target Option Is $200 Less Than The OG

June 2024 · 3 minute read

If you’re obsessed with Away, you’ll know the brand is pretty top-notch as far as luggage goes. Away bags are known for being sturdy, lightweight, and aesthetically pleasing. But if you’re not too keen on a hefty price tag of at least $275 for a carry-on suitcase, you might be in the same boat as us: hunting down an Away luggage dupe that’s nearly matched in quality, durability, and style.

This hardshell carry-on suitcase looks like a replica of the cult-favorite one from Away, and all seven of the colors—Muddy Aqua, Teal, Amber Brown, Gray, Light Pink, Lime Green and Beige—retail for just $90 each. When the O.G. costs upwards of $275, that’s an undeniable steal. On the interior, it has expandable sides, interior mesh compartments, and cross straps to secure your belongings. On the exterior, it has a telescoping handle for smooth handling and gliding and top and side handles for easy carrying.

Pick from seven neutral and playful shades that have a smooth, matte-looking finish. You get the same chic appearance of the Away suitcase but for a third of the price. At this point, you really shouldn’t have any hesitation in choosing a suitcase for all of your upcoming travels. I went ahead and bought one for myself, and can confirm that it is an incredible alternative for Away’s carry-on bag—I refuse to travel without it!

To get into the nitty-gritty details, both suitcases are almost the exact same weight and size, and both have near-identical capacities. And, Target’s suitcase weighs only 7.3 pounds itself, but can hold a whopping 100 pounds of belongings. That’s more than enough room for all of your clothing, toiletries, and souvenirs. So, it doesn’t take a rocket scientist to see that these two suitcases are as identical as Mary-Kate and Ashley and as strong as Superman himself. 

I bought mine right before moving across the country because I realized I didn’t have a solid carry-on bag that wasn’t a flimsy backpack or tote. With this rolling suitcase, I no longer have back cramps and aches, which is a true godsend when traveling. Though I’ve gotten just a handful of uses out of it so far, I can totally see it lasting me for years and years. In fact, I recently got my sister to buy one, too!

Even the Target reviews section is filled with glowing approvals of the luggage’s durability and stylish look. Take, for example, one shopper who confirms the dupe makes moving on the go an easier experience.

“Traveling can be exhausting, especially when you’re lugging around heavy bags,” they wrote. “That’s why I love this lightweight luggage. It’s made from durable materials that are surprisingly lightweight, making it a breeze to carry. I no longer have to worry about exceeding weight limits or straining my back while traveling. This luggage has truly made my trips much more enjoyable.”

“This luggage set is a true gem! The stylish design caught my eye, but it’s the functionality that truly impressed me,” said another.

Trust reviewers when they say this dupe lives up to its claims. Whether you’re planning your next trip or need a good gift idea, this carry-on suitcase is most definitely the way to go.
