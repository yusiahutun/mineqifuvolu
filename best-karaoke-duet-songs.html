30 Songs To Sing With A Partner

February 2024 · 5 minute read

Karaoke is great by yourself, but having friends to sing with is probably the best feeling in the world. Here are the best karaoke duet songs so that you and your partner can sing your hearts out.

We’re sure that these songs will fulfill all your needs no matter what genre it is and will spark different ideas so that you can add it to your setlist. As someone who goes to karaoke every week, it also takes some trial and error to know which songs will get the crowd riled up or will have your bestie’s heart light up with joy. Of course, there’s an endless list of options that’ll cater to different kinds of people. And hey, if you sing with someone else, no one will know if you messed up because in the end, it’s all about having fun!

So whether you’re singing with your bestie, your significant other, a stranger, or someone you just met (just like Troy and Gabriella from High School Musical), here are the 30 best karaoke songs you can sing on any occasion.

Best Karaoke Duet Songs

  • "Don't Go Breaking My Heart" By Elton John & Kiki Dee

    A 70s classic that you can sing with your partner or anyone who you have a loving relationship with.

  • "Breaking Free" from High School Musical

    Any Disney Channel kid will have this on their karaoke duet list. It’s a classic song that has affected generations with all the nostalgia.

  • "Shallow" by Lady Gaga and Bradley Cooper

    Be the Ally to someone’s Jackson Maine in this very theatrical song from A Star Is Born.

  • "Suddenly Seymour" from Little Shop Of Horrors

    A true musical theater classic that brings out all the romantic emotions derived from a monstrous plant.

  • "Crazy In Love" by Beyonce feat. Jay-Z

    Have a bond that’s as strong as Queen Bey and Jay-Z? Play it out with this early 2000s classic.

  • "Just Give Me A Reason" P!nk feat. Nate Ruess

    This song thrived on radio play, and if you’re someone who can belt like there’s no tomorrow, then this is the song for you.

  • "Leave The Door Open" by Silk Sonic

    You’ll be able to woo the entire the crowd with this very recent classic and you’ll be dripping the venue with finesse.

  • "Under Pressure" by Queen & David Bowie

    A classic ’70s anthem with a bassline that you would know anywhere. It’s sure to please everyone in the crowd.

  • "I Know What You Did Last Summer" by Shawn Mendes & Camila Cabello

    If you want something that’s a little bit different from “Señorita,” then look no further than another heartfelt Shawn and Camilla single.

  • "2 Minus 1" by SEVENTEEN

    As someone who goes to Koreatown noraebangs (karaoke rooms) all the time, I have a special place in my heart for singing K-pop songs. “2 Minus 1” is a certified banger with pop-punk influences and will get all of that heartache out in no time.

  • "WAP" by Cardi B & Megan Thee Stallion

    Want to make THEE statement at your next karaoke session? “WAP” is a fun time no matter how you’re feeling throughout the night.

  • "Prisoner" by Miley Cyrus & Dua Lipa

    If you wanna rock out and have that mysterious flaunt, look no further than Miley Cyrus and Dua Lipa’s iconic duet.

  • "Promiscuous" by Nelly Furtado ft. Timbaland

    A mid-2000s throwback and if you wanna have a fun and teasy convo with your partner, this is the song for you.

  • "We Don't Talk Anymore" by Charlie Puth ft. Selena Gomez

    Not gonna lie, the addictive beat and gentle fingerpicked guitars of “We Don’t Talk Anymore” had us singing since it came out.

  • "Best Part" Daniel Caesar & H.E.R.

    A chill song to sing with your loved one, and don’t forget the harmonies that are sweet and insane.

  • "TQG" by Shakira and Karol G

    They’re two of the biggest Latina artists in the world, and with an amazing beat and sultry lyrics, this song will get everyone hyped.

  • "Total Eclipse of The Heart" by Bonnie Tyler

    Do you want to feel super dramatic in an 80s way? Look no further than this classic by Bonnie Tyler.

  • "Somebody That I Used To Know" by Gotye feat. Kimbra

    This banger from 2010 will get all the suppressed emotions of heartache and wondering what your significant others are doing.

  • "The Phantom Of The Opera" from The Phantom of The Opera

    Sing for me! This Andrew Lloyd-Webber classic will amaze the crowd with it’s crazy vocal runs at the end.

  • "A Whole New World" from Aladdin

    This song will be the shining, shimmering, splendid musical act of the set.

  • "American Boy" by Estelle feat. Kanye West

    La-di-da. This chill song is impressive for any kind of geography you settle down with.

  • "Save Your Tears" The Weeknd & Ariana Grande

    You won’t be crying when you’re singing this song. It’s a relaxing song to sing with a partner in any situation (trust us).

  • "Stay" by Rihanna & Mikky Ekko

    A clear emotional portrait of wanting someone to be near you at all times. It is certainly the tearjerker of your karaoke set.

  • "Telephone" by Lady Gaga and Beyoncé

    We’re still waiting for a “Telephone Pt. 2,” Lady Gaga and Beyoncé. But, we’ll sing this one until our lungs give out.

  • "Beauty and a Beat" by Justin Bieber feat. Nicki Minaj

    A go-to song if you want all of that Bieber nostalgia.

  • "Closer" by The Chainsmokers feat. Halsey

    Hey, we won’t judge you for using this one. But it’s a good one on a night where you don’t know what the vibes are.

  • "Beg For You" by Charli XCX feat. Rina Sawayama

    This song is one of the best ones in 2022, and will maximize your joint slay with the biggest pop girlies out there.

  • "I Don't Wanna Live Forever" by Zayn & Taylor Swift

    With this, you’ll satisfy every Directioner and Swiftie in the crowd. Plus, it’s an amazing song.

  • "River Deep, Mountain High" from Glee

    We can’t forget this iconic Glee moment, and will spark joy in all the Gleeks in your life.

  • "Need You Now" by Lady A

    A very specific 2000s country song that will get you longing for someone who you can’t have.
