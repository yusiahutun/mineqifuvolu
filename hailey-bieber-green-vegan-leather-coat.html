Hailey Bieber Green Leather Coat

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Hailer Bieber’s style makes a statement. Even when she is not at a red-carpet premiere, glitzy shindig, or photo shoot, she is always dressed to impress. If anything, it is when she is galavanting around the Greater Los Angeles area with hubby Justin Bieber, grabbing coffee, or just chilling curbside that we really take notice of her outfits.

Indeed, being at the center of the paparazzi gaze, constantly fielding through crowds of photographers would prompt anyone to double-check their ensemble before walking out the door. And Bieber seemingly does that. Her ongoing stream of sartorial consciousness that mixes high and low pieces has us green with envy. Whether she owns or just borrows them from brands, few can say that they wouldn’t covet the items she wears.

BG002/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Bieber put this sentiment on full display yesterday, when she took a characteristic stroll through L.A. wearing an outfit that, in this instance, got the literal and figurative green light. She wore a long Kelly green vegan leather coat by Staud, a contemporary label founded by Sarah Staudinger. It featured pronounced pockets, a bow-like belt, and lining in a psychedelic print. These descriptors certainly pack a punch, which is perhaps why Bieber decided to match it with a simple white crop top, straight-leg jeans, Nike Air Force 1 sneakers, and a cross-body bag by Bottega Veneta.
