Alaska: The Last Frontier cast members profiles and updates

August 2024 · 6 minute read

Reality TV series Alaska: The Last Frontier cast includes individuals from the Kilcher extended family. They bring life and raw authenticity to the reality TV series, showcasing their remarkable resilience and connection to the harsh Alaska climate and rugged landscapes.

Alaska: The Last Frontier is a famous American reality cable TV series premiered in 2011. The show document the Kilcher family in the isolated community of Homer. For four generations, the Kilcher family have only lived off what their expansive 600-acre homestead provides. They hunt, farm, fish and gather other supplies from the land.

Alaska: The Last Frontier cast members

Alaska: The Last Frontier cast features members of the Kilcher family. Here are the most famous family members who have appeared in almost all Alaska: The Last Frontier episodes.

1. Atz Kilcher

Attila "Atz" Kilcher, born on 2nd September 1947, is the fourth of eight siblings and the oldest son of Ruth Weber and Yule Kilcher. He is one of the most famous stars in Alaska: The Last Frontier, which focuses on Kilcher’s homestead on Kachemak Bay.

2. Otto Kilcher

Otto Kilcher is the sixth of eight Kilcher siblings. He is an American reality television personality and mechanic. Born in Alaska, Otto was brought up on their family's farm raising cattle. Otto, one of the successful stars of Alaska: The Last Frontier cast net worth is about $4 million.

He started showing interest in machinery at a young age; he could collect and repair broken machinery. His hobby pushed him to build a small store of parts which he used to fix anything that broke on their farm. Today, he is a master mechanic and machinist.

3. Charlotte Kilcher

Charlotte Kilcher, originally from Northern California, has been married to Otto for over 20 years.

Looking for adventure, Charlotte, a pioneer-minded lady, went to Alaska in 1978 to work as a biologist. She and Otto Kilcher met while helping address an ecological disaster. Charlotte and Otto have two sons, August and Torrey, and two step-sons from Otto, Elvin and Levi.

4. Atz Lee Kilcher

Atz Lee Kilcher is Atz's son and the younger brother of singer Jewel. The experienced hiker has always considered himself the family's black sheep. Years ago, he left the subsistence life of his family looking for a different experience but came back. He has been on the show since its premiere in 2011.

Atz Lee Kilcher and his ex-wife Jane have a daughter Piper and a son Etienne.

5. Eivin Kilcher

Eivin Kilcher is a 3rd generation homesteader who was taught how to be self-reliant and resourceful. By the time he was an adult, Eivin had naturally learned the essential skills to survive and thrive in their wild land.

Like his grandfather, Eivin Kilcher started with a single cabin and expanded his field/home he now shares with his beautiful wife, Eve, two adorable children, Findlay and Sparrow Rose and their cute dog.

He is a former firefighter skilled in knitting, welding, knife making, excavating, etc. In his spare time, Eivin Kilcher co-authored a cookbook Homestead Kitchen: Stories and Recipes from Our Hearth to Yours, with his beautiful wife, Eve. The book lists 85 original family recipes and advice on foraging, preserving and gardening.

6. Jane Kilcher

Jane Kilcher is originally from Homer, Alaska, near the famous Kilcher homestead. She spent most of her years as a commercial fisherwoman.

But her love for music landed her in Atz Lee's arms; she left fishing and relocated to the Kilcher homestead. However, Jane and Atz Lee are now divorced; she shared the unfortunate news on her social media account.

7. Eve Kilcher

Eve was raised in rural Alaska, while in this wilderness, she naturally learned to be self-reliant, pragmatic, and respect nature. You've interacted with Eve's personality if you watch Alaska: The Last Frontier. She is a lovely woman who inspires others to appreciate and enjoy life.

Eve loves gardening, making cheese, baby goats, foraging for food and discovering new ways to decorate and maintain her daughter’s beautiful wild hair. She spends most of her time in the kitchen and teaches her children to respect and love the homestead life.

8. August Kilcher

August Kilcher is the son of Otto and Charlotte Kilcher. The reality star is famous for starring in Alaska: The Last Frontier. He is a survivalist and naturalist, and he documents his life as a homesteader and shares the content on social media.

What happened to August on Alaska: The Last Frontier? After high school, August Kilcher left the historic homestead and enrolled in college in the lower 48. But realising he wasn't meant for a more conventional lifestyle, he returned to the homestead.
