Beauty mogul Huda Kattan gives sister a dramatic makeover

July 2024 · 4 minute read

As the woman at the helm of a sprawling beauty empire, Huda Kattan is normally the one testing out treatments in front of the camera.

But in a rare role reversal, the makeup mogul has thrust her sister Alya, 44, into the spotlight, by helping her to undergo a makeover of dramatic proportions.

The pair say they are both delighted with the results of the image overhaul which cost an eye-watering $15,500 (£11,744) and included cheek fillers, Botox and various laser treatments.

The Dubai-based siblings focused on four main areas - hair, face, body and eyebrows - and have left fans amazed by Alya's transformation.

Huda, who boasts a staggering 21 million Instagram followers, told how her sister - a 'low maintenance kinda gal' who doesn't focus on her appearance - had begged her for a revamp.

Transformation: Alya Kattan, 44, shows off the results of her dramatic makeover (right) - including new hair, cheek fillers, laser eye surgery and eyebrow microblading

Sharing the spotlight: Beauty tycoon Huda Kattan *(pictured) told how her sister - a 'low maintenance kinda gal' who doesn't focus on her appearance - wanted an image overhaul

Writing on her blog, the 33-year-old beauty tycoon said: 'I'm a firm believer in the power of makeup; its transformative abilities to give you confidence and change the way you feel about yourself.

'Of course, I couldn't wait to help her feel more beautiful and see her more confident!'

Alya commented: 'I was very unkempt and I really didn't feel good about myself. I needed a drastic transformation and Huda was the perfect person to help me.'

Speaking afterwards, Alya added: 'I was feeling really really bad about myself, and this makeover has made me feel SO much better.

'I never believed it before, but looking good can actually have a huge impact on how you feel about yourself.' 

Hair 

Alya's hair transformation was worth an eye-watering $5000 (£3,787) in total

Salon: Mèche in Beverly Hills, CA

Cost: $5,000 (£3,787) 

Stylist Sarah Brown, who was recommended by Jen Atkin, went 'all out' and added super long extensions (22 pieces of 20-inch extensions).

She then added soft honey tones to warm up her complexion and give Alya the ‘J-Lo look’ she wanted.

Between bleaching, Alya got a keratin treatment to improve the strength of the hair before the color was added. 

Finally, Alya received a Brazilian Blowout to eliminate frizz and boost shine, which maintains the health of the hair and lasts for around 12 weeks.

Alya was so happy with the final result of her hair, she was intent on calling her transformation 'ratchet to Rapunzel' according to Huda.

Face and body 

Alya swapped her 'chipmunk cheeks' for a rounded, more flattering visage

Salon: Beauty Park Medical Spa in Santa Monica, CA

Expert: Nurse Jamie

Cost: $8,000 (£6,061)  

Alya previously had cheek fillers, but they weren't in the right place, so as Alya puts it, they gave her the appearance of 'chipmunk' cheeks. 

She had fillers, Botox, various laser treatments for face, SculpSure for her body and pixel laser therapy for face. 

The laser creates thousands of microscopic perforations in the surface of the skin, which prompts the skin to produce new collagen, leaving the skin smoother and more even-toned.

Eyebrows 

Alya has also had her eyebrows done along with laser eye surgery and a mani

Salon: Microblading LA in Woodland Hills, CA

Cost: $2,500 (£1,894) 

Brow artist Lindsey Ta added fullness to Alya's eyebrows and lifted them slightly by raising the outer arch for a more youthful and fresh look.

To complete her new look, Alya got laser eye surgery so she no longer needs to wear contact lenses.

Finally, Huda convinced her of the need for regular manicures saying: 'Maintenance is something Alya hates, so she’ll usually get her mani and pedi every two to three weeks.

'[Other sister] Mona and I have finally persuaded her to come for weekly pamper sessions at the Dollhouse with us.'
