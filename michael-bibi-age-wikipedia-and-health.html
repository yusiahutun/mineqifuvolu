On June 5, Michael Bibi made the tragic statement that he had recently been diagnosed with CNS lymphoma, a rare type of cancer that impacts the brain and spine.

An uncommon form of cancer known as CNS lymphoma targets primarily the lymph tissue in the brain and/or spinal cord. He has started a tough and demanding treatment journey as a result of this diagnosis.
