Whatever happened to the American Beauty babes?

May 2024 · 3 minute read

While Kevin Spacey and Annette Bening were already well-known, its teenage stars Thora Birch and Mena Suvari earned international recognition from the drama.

Thora played Lester Burnham's awkward daughter Jane, while Mena played Angela Hayes, the blonde cheerleader Jane's father fell in love with.

Both ladies were praised for their performances and were expected to become superstars – but 16 years on, things haven't quite worked out that way.

So where are the American Beauty babes now?

THORA BIRCH

Well, hold tight folks, because Thora's disappearance from the spotlight is a tad more bizarre than your usual career fail.

Miss Birch was already a successful child star, appearing in everybody's favourite Halloween movie Hocus Pocus alongside Bette Midler and in the coming-of-age film Now And Then.

After American Beauty, she starred in hits including horror The Hole and Ghost World, which she gained a Golden Globe nomination for.

But Thora hasn't starred in a hit in years, with her last film being the self-produced 2012 movie Petunia, which had a limited release – and that's all down to her father.

Thora's dad is Jack Birch, a porn star who appeared in the legendary adult movie Deep Throat, and he's more than a bit controlling when it comes to his daughter's career.

In American Beauty, he had to supervise his daughter's topless scene because at 16, she was underage – but still wanted to be on set during her sex scenes when she was older.

During the filming of Winter Of Frozen Dreams, Jack insisted that he supervise Thora's sex scenes, before giving the actor who was pretending to bed his daughter a thumbs up and threatening to kill the assistant directors.

Jack then managed to get Thora axed from a Broadway production of Dracula when he threatened another actor for rubbing Birch's back during a scene.

Finally, Thora was sacked from Manson Girls, with director Susanna Lo saying: "There have been several problems with her father/manager that make it too difficult to work things out in an ensemble cast situation."

Now 33, Thora last starred on the big screen in 2009, and has no upcoming projects listed. We're sure family dinners aren't awkward at all.

MENA SUVARI

Mena had a bit more luck than her castmate, but still isn't exactly dominating Hollywood.

The beauty started her career in TV shows including Boy Meets World and ER, before progressing to the big screen.

1999 was a good year for Suvari – as well as starring in American Beauty, she nabbed a role in the iconic teen movie American Pie, and also returned for the sequel two years later.

The next few years brought several projects, including Loser and the video for Wheatus' Teenage Dirtbag, with Mena later landing a seven episode stint in Six Feet Under.

However, the work isn't exactly pouring in for Mena, who is now 36.

After divorcing two husbands, she starred in yet another American Pie sequel in 2012.

But Miss Suvari could be in for a comeback – she's reportedly nabbed the lead role in a paranormal TV series called South Of Hell.

Maybe the girls can try get some guest appearances on Kevin Spacey's House Of Cards?
