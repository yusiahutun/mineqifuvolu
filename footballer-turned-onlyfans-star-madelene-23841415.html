Footballer turned OnlyFans star Madelene Wright hires personal coach to relaunch career

April 2024 · 2 minute read

OnlyFans star Madelene Wright has hired a personal football coach to relaunch her career.

The former Charlton Athletic Women's player was sacked by her club after controversial videos of her appeared on social media last December.

She has since joined subscription service OnlyFans and spent months in Dubai showing off her stunning figure, but now she is keen to get her football career back on track.

The ex-Millwall ace reported earlier this week that she is set to return to the pitch, and now it appears she has hired coach Luke Jones for someone to one training to help get her back up to speed.

Madelene wrote on Instagram: "Last couple of days training has been full of mixed emotions. I've loved the excitement of being back but the frustration of not being as clean and sharp as before has been difficult.

"Good to be working with @lj121footballfit to help me get back to the standard of playing I need to be at.

"Looking forward to working hard the next couple of months."

The blonde bombshell parted ways with Charlton after the Daily Star uncovered videos of her appearing to inhale from a balloon at a party and later swigging from a bottle while driving her Range Rover.

Madelene later admitted her "mistakes" on social media as Charlton confirmed her registration had been cancelled last December.

Soon afterwards the 22-year-old jetted off to Dubai where she joined OnlyFans, charging her subscribers £24 per month for exclusive pictures and videos.

Her page has the tagline" "Angel face. Devil thoughts."

She teased the content with a steady stream of skimpy bikini shots while she was in the Middle East.

But earlier this week Madelene confirmed she was heading back to the football after returning to the UK.

She wrote: "Start training again tomorrow. Nervous but excited."
