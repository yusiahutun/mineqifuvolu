Basketball is back, but first is the NBA Play-In Tournament, which decides the final teams in the NBA Playoffs. Wondering how to watch the NBA Play-In Tournament? Well, we have the recommendation for the best and one of the cheapest ways to stream the start of NBA season and it won’t break the bank.

The NBA Play-In Tournament is a series of games that determine the NBA teams that fill the seventh and eighth playoff seeds in both the Western and Eastern conferences for the 2024 NBA Playoffs. While the NBA teams that finish number 1 to 6 in the standings of each conference are guaranteed a playoff spot, the teams that finish number 7 to 10 in standings must compete in the Play-In Tournament to determine who will secure the seventh and eighth playoff seeds.

Here’s how the NBA Play-In Tournament works. Each conference’s number 7 team in the standings will host the number 8 team. The winner will secure the number 7 seed in the NBA Playoffs, while the loser will get another chance to earn a playoff spot. Then, each conference’s number 9 team in the standings will host the number 10 team. The winner will advance to the final stage of the Play-In Tournament, while the losers are eliminated. The losers of the number 7 vs. number 8 game will host the winners of the number 9 vs. number 10 games. The winners will secure the number 8 seed in the NBA playoffs for their respective conferences, while the losing teams will be eliminated.

When is the NBA Play-In Tournament 2024?

The NBA Play-In Tournament will be held from Tuesday, April 16, to Friday, April 19, a day before the first round of the NBA Playoffs start. See the NBA Play-In Tournament schedule for the Western and Eastern Conferences below.

Tuesday, April 16, 2024 – Western Conference

  • Los Angeles Lakers vs. New Orleans Pelicans @ 7:30 p.m. ET on TNT
  • Golden State Warriors vs. Sacramento Kings @ 10 p.m. ET on TNT

Wednesday, April 17, 2024 – Eastern Conference

  • Miami Heats vs. Philadelphia 76ers @ 7 p.m. ET on ESPN
