Mary Kay's Updated TimeWise Miracle Set Reduces Even More Fine Lines &amp; Wrinkles

March 2024 · 2 minute read

When Mary Kay rolled out its original TimeWise Miracle Set in 2018, the beauty industry noticed. With patented ingredients to help reduce premature skin aging, the products garnered a cult following. Now, almost six years later, Mary Kay updated the TimeWise Miracle Set to be even more efficacious, for fewer visible fine lines and wrinkles. Plus, there’s a new edition to the team. Who says you can’t change a good thing?

With the help of Mary Kay scientist Dr. Lucy Gildea, the brand made some incremental but impactful improvements. Fans of the original products recognize the hero ingredients: the patented TimeWise 3D Complex, which is still in each product. It promises to help protect against and counteract environmental stressors that cause premature skin aging.

The new-and-improved TimeWise Miracle Set contains the ophthalmologist-tested TimeWise 4-In-1 Cleanser; TimeWise Antioxidant Moisturizer for non-greasy, all-day hydration; TimeWise Daytime Defender SPF 30 Broad Spectrum Sunscreen to protect against UVA/UVB rays; and the brand-new TimeWise Nighttime Recovery with chamomile extract to help calm and soothe skin.

Choose from Normal/Dry or Combination/Oily.

The new formula, and with the addition of TimeWise Nighttime Recovery in the routine, Mary Kay promises fewer fine lines, increased firmness, improved radiance, added hydration, improved skin texture, and a better overall appearance. All products are oil- and fragrance-free, safe for sensitive skin, and noncomedogenic so they won’t clog pores.

In fact, in an independent, third-party clinical or consumer study in which from 55 to 214 women used a TimeWise Miracle Set product as directed, panelists say increased hydration and soft skin in one day, fading fine lines in two weeks, improved radiance in four weeks, and more resilient skin in five weeks. Pretty major.

For a full-face routine, add TimeWise Eye Cream ($34 at Mary Kay), which can be found in the new Ultimate TimeWise Miracle Set ($150 at Mary Kay). Aging is a privilege but you can decide how exactly you do it.
