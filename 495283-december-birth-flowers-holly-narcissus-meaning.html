December birth flowers, Holly and Narcissus, and their meaning

April 2024 · 7 minute read

If you are lucky, you may have received a birth flower to commemorate a special occasion, such as a birthday or anniversary. Generally, a birth flower is associated with a particular month of the year. Each month has a unique flower related to it, which often has special meanings and symbolism. For example, the Carnation is January's birth flower, symbolising love, fascination, and distinction. But what are the December birth flowers?

December is a special month for many reasons. However, one of the unique aspects of the month is the birth of two special flowers - Holly and Narcissus. These December birth flowers have deep and meaningful symbolism and are often chosen to represent the birth of a new year or a new life.

The two birth flowers represent faithfulness, good wishes, respect, happiness, optimism, good fortune, and peace. The flowers are often given as gifts to people born in December to wish them well and bring them joy.

What is the symbolism of the Narcissus flower?

Narcissus is the main flower of the month in December. Also known as the daffodil or jonquil, the Narcissus is a beautiful yellow flower with various special meanings and symbolism. First, the flower is a symbol of beauty and vanity. Besides beauty and vanity, the Narcissus flower is often associated with joy, hope, and new beginnings.

Its bright yellow colour is seen as a sign of sunshine, cheerfulness, and optimism, and its unique beauty has made this month of December flower a favourite among gardeners and flower lovers alike. The Narcissus flower is also believed to bring good fortune and luck to those born in December, and its sweet scent is said to bring comfort and joy to all who smell it.

The Narcissus flower has a long and fascinating history in Greek mythology. The flower is named after the mythological character Narcissus, who fell in love with his reflection in a pool. The gods punished him for his vanity by turning him into the Narcissus flower. Thus, the flower symbolises vanity, pride, redemption, and hope.

People also associate the Narcissus flower with the Roman goddess Proserpina, often depicted with a daffodil in her hand. Proserpina was said to be the goddess of the underworld and the goddess of spring. Thus, the Narcissus flower was seen as a sign of the start of the spring season.

The birth flower in December represents truth and honesty. Therefore, people perceive it as a sign of reliability and trustworthiness and that it can bring out the best in those born in December.

What is the symbolism of the Holly flower?

The Holly flower is a popular birth flower for those born in December. It is associated with the old English name 'Holly' and its Latin name is Ilex aquifolium. The flower symbolises luck and good fortune; many believe it brings good luck to anyone born under its sign.

Holly is a deep green shrub with bright red berries and has long been associated with Christmas and the winter solstice. In many cultures, it is seen as a symbol of hope and renewal. For example, Christians believe the flower symbolises the nails used in the Crucifixion of Jesus Christ. Thus, they use it to decorate homes and gardens during Christmas.

Do December birth flowers have any joint significance?

Why does December have 2 flowers?

The month only has these two flowers because they represent the dual nature of the month. In other words, December is a holiday season in most cultures. While December is a time of joy and celebration, it is also a time of reflection and contemplation.

Holly is associated with the winter solstice, which marks the beginning of winter. Narcissus is associated with the end of winter, signifying the hope of warmer days ahead. Therefore, taken together, the two flowers represent the duality of the month: the joy of the holiday season and the hope for a brighter future.

What does December's birth flower look like?

The birth flower for December is the Holly and Narcissus. The Holly is an evergreen shrub with glossy, dark green leaves and red berries. On the other hand, the Narcissus is a genus of perennial flowering plants with fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers.

The flowers come in various colours, including white, yellow, orange and pink. The Holly and Narcissus symbolise good wishes, hope, faithfulness and respect.

Is December a Poinsettia or Narcissus flower?

Generally, Narcissus is the flower for the month of December. However, Poinsettia, specifically the Paperwhite flower, is commonly associated with Christmas and can be a birth flower for December.

People prefer to call the Narcissus the flower for December, while the Poinsettia is the Christmas flower or the Christmas star. The Poinsettia is known for its vibrant red and green foliage. People associate it with joy and good cheer, making it a perfect choice for Christmas.

When can you plant Holly and Narcissus flowers?

You can plant Holly and Narcissus flowers in late autumn or early winter, after the first frost when the soil has cooled off. The best practice is to plant them in a sunny spot with well-draining soil.

The flowers need a period of cold weather to blossom properly. Therefore, you should wait to plant them after the danger of frost has passed. When planting the bulbs, plant them deep enough to make their tips visible. Water them thoroughly and keep the soil moist throughout the growing season.
