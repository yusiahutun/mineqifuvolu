Joaquin Phoenix goes off-script to 'grunt like a pig' on all fours in Napoleon sex scene

October 2024 · 3 minute read

Napoleon star Joaquin Phoenix got down on all fours and squealed “like a little pig” in one of the year's weirdest sex scenes – and it wasn’t in the script.

The 49-year-old Academy Award winner played titular character, French military commander Napoleon Bonaparte, in Ridley Scott’s romanticised biography. Amongst the breath-taking battle scenes and captivating drama, another thing stood out: the film’s kooky sex scenes.

“Sex can get boring,” the Gladiator director said on IndieWire’s Filmmaker Toolkit podcast on Monday. So, in order to spice up the onscreen bonking, Ridley, 86, always kept at least four cameras rolling during intimate scenes to allow the actors to freely express themselves.
