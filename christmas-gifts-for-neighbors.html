8

Melted Ornaments

This craft is fun to do with your kids to gift to their neighbor friends. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Grease parchment paper and insides of cookie cutters. Set cutters on baking sheet; fill with a single layer of mints in bottom, breaking to fill any gaps. Bake at 350 degrees just until melted, about 5 to 6 minutes. Let stand 2 minutes, then poke a small hole in top. Let stand 5 minutes; remove cutters. Thread a piece of twine or ribbon through the hole to hang.
