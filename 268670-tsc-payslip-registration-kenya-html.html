    7 mistakes that you should avoid while doing your TSC payslip registration

    Facing errors while registering for your TSC payslips online? Well, you will not anymore because here are the mistakes that you can definitely avoid and get your registration done at the very first try.

    1. Do not enter invalid credentials

    Invalid details should be avoided. However, if you are trying for TSC payslip login/register with a valid name, ensure that the names start with a capital letter followed by the remaining in small letters.

    2. Avoid improper scan and upload the certificates

    Certified copies of professional certificates and academic certificates are required. The process also requires the inclusion of the national identity card along with a passport size photo. Now, if the photos or the files are improperly scanned and uploaded, the registration will be cancelled.

    3. Do not delay depositing the registration fee

    The authorities already provide the bank name, account name, and number, plus M-Pesa details, so they expect an on-time payment. Delay in the payment is thus not acceptable, and the registration might turn to be void.

    4. Mistakes while entering bank account is risky

    Since they have already provided the details for their bank account, entering the wrong details will result in the transfer of money to a different account. This will not only delay your registration but cost you money.

    5. Avoid fraudulent activities

    All the components in the registration for TSC payslip are strictly mandatory, and the applicants must provide accurate information about their credentials. Immediate de-registration might occur if fraudulent activities are encountered.

    6. Do not forget to check your registration status

    Double-check if your registration has been successful. Go for a detailed survey if all the credentials have been properly and accurately used for your registration.

    7. Try to avoid agencies/consultancy for your registration

    You should not use agencies, consultancies, or middlemen for registration purposes. There may be offices advertising, Online TSC payslip, Payslip online TSC, and such. Be careful of such a hoax. Your credentials might be used for unethical malpractices that will cause you trouble in the future.

    FAQ on TSC payslip online

    If you're interested in TSC payslip online registration, you probably have a few questions. You're not alone. Here are answers to other common questions on online payslip TSC.

    How do I check my TSC payslip online?

    How do I log into my TSC portal?

    After successful registration, you can log in and access payslip online using your TSC number and password.

    How do I access my TSC payslip online?

    You need to download the document before you can view it. So, how do I download my TSC payslip online?

  • Log into the main TSC portal
  • Go to the Online Services tab
  • Click the Online Payslips option
  • You will be prompted to enter your username and password to log in
  • When you log into the system, you will be redirected you to the TSC home page with the dashboard section
  • Go to "View payslip" menu
  • Click on the"View payslip" alternative to download and see your online payslips

    • Always log out of your account once you are done using the service for security and confidentiality reasons.

    What are the contents of TSC payslip?

    A TSC payslip sample contains basic information such as month and year, the teacher's TSC number, name, ID number, KRA PIN, job group, station, date of retirement, and terms of employment. It also contains the PayPoint, basic salary, allowances, total earnings, third party deductions, total deductions, and the net pay for the specific month.
