D’Von Dudley | Early Life, Family & Education 

D’Von Dudley was born in Huntington, New York, to parents Preston Dudley and Renee Washington.

His father, Preston, was a bishop, and his mother, Renee Washington, is a pastor.

As for education, D’Von attended New Rochelle High School. As a teenager, he played football throughout the school.

After his graduation, D’Von Dudley went on to pursue his career in wrestling.

After his training session with Johnny Rodz in 1996, D’Von Dudley finally got his break as D-Von, the evil Dudley Brother.

D’Von Dudley then soon partnered with Bubba Ray Dudley, who was a newcomer as well.

The duo later went on to form The Dudley Boyz, which became one of the most successful tag teams in the history of ECW.

Furthermore, the tag team goes on to win the respected ECW World Tag team titles eight times.

After his successive wrestling career, D’Von Dudley established a smoothie shop. The 3D’s Poer Blends opened in Melbourne, Florida, and flourished under his guidance. 

Furthermore, D’Von Dudley collaborates with Brother Ray to open Team 3D, an Academy of Professional Wrestling and Sports Entertainment located in Kissimmee, Florida.

D’von Dudley | Professional Career

Devon Hughes or D’von Dudley is a widely renowned American semi-retired professional wrestler.

Recognized by his ring name as Brother Devon spent a successful spell in WEE. D’Von Dudley wrestled for ECW or Extreme Championship Wrestling from the year 1995 to 1999.

Besides, he was also a part of the wrestler roaster for WWF/E or World Wrestling Federation/Entertainment from 1999 to 2005. 

Later in his career, D’Von Dudley performed as Brother Devon for TNA (Total Nonstop Action Wrestling) from 2005 to 2014.

Hughes, along with his tag team partner Mark LoMonaco is one-half of the tag team known as Team 3D or the Dudley Boyz.

The duo is characterized by the usage of tables and unorthodox ring apparel in their matches. Team 3D is the most successful and iconic tag team in professional wrestling.

Likewise, Team 3D has also won over 23 world tag team championships in TNA. The duo is also the first tag team that could be inducted into the TNA Hall of Fame.

Moreover, D’Von Dudley has held 25 major championship belts between ECW, TNA, and NJPW (Ne Japan Pro Wrestling).
