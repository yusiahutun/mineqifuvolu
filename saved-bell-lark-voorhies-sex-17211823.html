Riddle of Saved By The Bell sex tape: 'Freaky' home video leak imminent

September 2024 · 2 minute read

But now, another Bayside High alumni is facing their home movie going viral.

Lark Voorhies, who played Lisa Turtle on the classic 90s show, is facing a sex tape battle after her estranged husband threatened to sell sexual footage of the star.

The 41-year-old's husband, Jimmy Green – who Lark filed from divorce from last week – reportedly told TMZ that he had a sex tape of him and his wife, and that he wanted to sell it to the highest bidder.

However, Lark told the website that if he does have a sex tape, it was filmed without her knowledge.

Jimmy claims that Voorhies knew about the filming, adding that it was filmed on her phone.

He told Radar that the tape is "freaky", saying: "Anybody who sees it would be surprised. Ain’t nobody seen Lark like that! We don’t do nothing regular. The tape shows everything."

The music engineer said that he would consider selling the tape for $2 million.

Lark met Jimmy last year and married in April after eloping to Las Vegas.

Last week, the actress filed for divorce due to "irreconcilable differences".

If a sex tape does leak, this will be the second Saved By The Bell video to surface.

In 2006, Dustin Diamond directed and released his own tape called Screeched: Saved By The Smell (yes, we know, childhood-ruining).

Since then, however, he has claimed that his likeness was added to the video later and he used a stunt double for the threesome sex scenes.
