Cast of 'Everybody Loves Raymond': Catch Up With Them

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Ever since the Barone family appeared on our screens in 1996, we just could not help but fall in love with them. From their quick comebacks, hilarious antics, and overall just wholesome family fun, tuning into this show was definitely a highlight. Which were we decided to see what the cast of Everybody Loves Raymond has been up to since the show ended after 210 heartwarming and hilarious episodes in 2005. 

Related: Catch Up With the Cast of ‘Buffy the Vampire Slayer’ — See the Stars Then and Now!

While the show was on the air, it won 15 Primetime Emmys, 56 wins, and 177 total nominations. It followed sports writer Ray Barone (Ray Romano) and how he navigates his wife, children, parents, brother, and sister-in-law, who all happen to either live with him or just across the street. His dad is always raiding his fridge, his mom is always meddling in his life, and his brother just seems mad at him all the time. But somehow, Ray still manages to make it work with love, respect, and humor. 

 Fans of the show can stream it on Peacock and Paramount+.
