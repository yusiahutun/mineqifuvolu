13 Best Places to Get Heart Shaped Pizza 2024

October 2024 · 3 minute read
1

Fresh Brothers

Fresh Brothers

California's Fresh Brothers is offering 14-inch heart-shaped pizzas for the same price as their original crust pizzas for the month of February.

2

Papa John's

Papa John's

Papa John's is bringing back their Heart-Shaped Pizza nationwide (February 7-14). The one-topping thin crust Heart-Shaped Pizza is offered at $11.99 and comes unsliced. Rewards members who order on Valentine's Day will earn free Oreo Cookie Papa Bites on their next $25+ purchase (must use before February 26).

3

Papa Murphy's

Polara Studio

Papa Murphy's HeartBaker Pizza is back this year at participating locations. Choose from cheese or pepperoni for $11. Want dessert too? The Sweetheart of a Deal, which includes a Heartbaker Pizza and Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough, costs $14. (Available through February 15.)

4

Hungry Howie's

Hungry Howie

Hungry Howie's is delivering an inexpensive Valentine's Day option again this year. Their heart-shaped one-topping pizzas and heart shaped 3-Cheeser Howie Bread cost just $7.99 each. Available February 13-14 at participating locations.

5

Marco's Pizza

Getty Images

Marco’s Pizza is again offering a heart-shaped pizza this year (Feb. 10-14) for $9.99 with 1-topping (prices and participation may vary). Use code "HEART" when you order online.

6

California Pizza Kitchen

California Pizza Kitchen

CPK is bringing back their heart-shaped thin-crust pizzas for a limited time (February 9-14). Choose from a variety of popular pizzas such as The Original BBQ Chicken Pizza and Mushroom Pepperoni Sausage Pizza (dine-in only).

The restaurant is also offering a Sweet Deal Menu for Two starting at $40-$50 that includes selections of an appetizer, two entrées, and one dessert off their prix fixe menu.

7

Round Table Pizza

Round Table Pizza

Round Table Pizza, with locations in California, Arizona, Nevada, and Washington, is offering heart-shaped pizzas again this this year. Their specialty and make-your-own pies are available through in store or online order (valid through February 28). You can also get a $5 medium pizza with one topping (dine-in, carryout, and delivery) on Feb. 14 with a purchase of a large specialty pizza at regular price with the code LOVE5.

8

Mountain Mike's Pizza

Mountain Mike's Pizza

West Coast chain Mountain Mike's Pizza is offering heart-shaped pizzas topped with their signature curly pepperoni for the whole month of February ($22.99).

9

Lou Malnati's Lou Malnati's Heart-Shaped Pizza Package

9

Lou Malnati's Lou Malnati's Heart-Shaped Pizza Package

Credit: Amazon

The famous Chicago restaurant offers their heart-shaped pizzas for sale through Amazon. This package includes one heart-shaped cheese and one round sausage Chicago-style deep dish pizza. Lou Malnati's bakes the pizzas and then flash-freezes them, and you simply reheat them in the oven.

10

Peter Piper Pizza

Peter Piper Pizza

Peter Piper Pizza has locations in California, Texas, Arizona, and New Mexico. Their Sweetheart Special includes a 1-topping heart-shaped pizza and large crunch dessert for just $19.99 (through February 21).

11

Pizza Hut

Pizza Hut

Pizza Hut offers heart-shaped hand-tossed crust pizzas this year from February 8-14. Prices start at $12.99. Or sweeten the deal and add an Ultimate Chocolate Chip Cookie or Triple Chocolate Brownie.

12

Mickey's Italian Deli & Pizzeria

Mickey's

If you happen to be in the Hermosa Beach, California, area, look no further than Mickey's Pizza. Prices begin at $20, with additional toppings costing $1.50.

13

Your Own Kitchen!

Denis Tevekov

If you want to cook this year, make a heart-shaped pizza yourself! Many grocery stores like Whole Foods and Publix offer fresh pizza dough for sale. Simply shape the dough into a heart, add any toppings your heart (heh) desires, and bake as directed.
