Elle Brooke's anguish as she's forced to pick between OnlyFans and boxing career

Having risen to fame over the past couple of years, Elle Brooke is undeniably one of the UK adult entertainment industry's biggest players right now.

However, like many internet stars over the past few years, she's caught the boxing bug, and doesn't want to stop her training any time soon.

With lightning hands and brutal power, the porn star has demolished every fighter who's dared set foot in the ring with her.

Brooke responded: "That's a really good question. A really good question. Probably model just for the fact that it's so much easier than boxing.

"But, obviously, I do love boxing. I don't want to say it's a side hustle, because I spend so much time doing it, but it goes alongside my job really well at the moment."

Not satisfied with her answer, another one of her 766K followers asked: "Would you rather give up boxing or Only Fans?"

The model answered: "You guys are obsessed with this question; I just answered one that's basically the same. I got this question so many times, more than 20 times. Guys, I do love boxing, but the money is nowhere near the same as what I make on Only Fans; it's actually f***ing crazy. I do it for the love of the sport."
