The Original Cheetor

Cheetor, one of the most beloved characters from the Beast Wars: Transformers series, made his debut in the first episode titled “Beast Wars, Part 1.” He was originally portrayed as a young and impulsive Maximal who transformed into a sleek and agile cheetah.

Cheetor’s beast mode as a cheetah allowed him to achieve impressive speeds, making him the fastest among the Maximals. With his sleek design and hyper-advanced technology, Cheetor could outrun almost any opponent.

Cheetor was the first Maximal to undergo the Transmetal upgrade, which transformed his cheetah mode into a more metallic and futuristic version. This upgrade not only enhanced his physical abilities but also added a set of rocket boosters to his arsenal.
