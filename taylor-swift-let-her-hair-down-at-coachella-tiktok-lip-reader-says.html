Taylor Swift let her hair down at Coachella, TikTok 'lip-reader' says

January 2024 · 3 minute read

Tay Tay ditched her celebrity cloak when she enjoyed herself over the weekend with her boyfriend, a popular TikTok star claims. The unofficial lip-reader says Taylor Swift let her hair down at Coachella, as videos continue to storm social media.

Taylor Swift being at Coachella with Travis Kelce was not on our bingo card, and according to a TikTok star who has experience as a lip-reader, she didn’t expect certain aspects from the weekend either. The Grammy-award winning singer was definitely making sure she took advantage of her break from the Eras Tour by hitting up the festival the other day. Walking in with beau Travis Kelce, Taylor not only sported her boyfriend’s merch, but danced the night away without a care in the world. The celebrity appeared to be on Cloud Nine.

TikTok star ‘lip-reader’ gives her take on Taylor Swift’s time at Coachella

TikTok star Tis Me Jackie G spends her time raising awareness about deafness, and her bio says “Your local deafie”.

She also participated in the viral Netflix real-life Squid Game as contestant 393, but sadly didn’t walk away with the cash prize. Jackie shares her arts and crafts with her fans, having her own website, as well as signing to popular songs that are trending on the app.

The TikToker has also taken her guess, by acting the part of a lip-reader, to suggest what Taylor Swift was saying in the viral clips of Coachella from the weekend.

While Jackie doesn’t work as an official lip-reader, she does have experience. This is her take.

Jackie scoped many clips, and says after Taylor and Travis exited the VIP section to head into the pit, she was over the moon.

“Oh my God, we’re in the front?” She may have said.

The TikToker also believes, after she watched Ice Spice – her gal pal – she was having the time of her life.

“That was the best thing that’s happened tonight. Sorry I’m drunk”, Jackie thinks Taylor says.

As with all lip readers, only Taylor Swift knows what she said during her night out at Coachella, but Swifties don’t mind if they just have videos of her instead.

‘This is Travis’

Another video though where Taylor is meeting someone in the crowd has been highlighted.

Taylor is said to have begun with: “This is Travis”, as she gestures to her NFL tight end towering over her to someone nearby who shakes their hand.

“Hi, it’s nice to meet you”, it looks like she adds.

But this is one thing that fans love about the music star, over how she when she introduces herself, she never gives her full name. “Hi, I’m Taylor,” she often says, dropping the celebrity vibe and being more down to Earth.

Here’s a compilation showing it, especially how even during her concerts she’s on a first-name basis.

Now, she has the same approach for Travis – dropping the Kelce, one of the most famous tight ends in NFL history, Super Bowl champ and New Heights podcast host from the introduction.

Even though they are the most recognizable celebrity couples on the planet right now, Hollywood hasn’t changed them much.

One fan wrote: “I love that she only says Taylor and not Taylor Swift”.

Someone else replied: “Me too! So personal!”

Another said: “I kind of love that she still does it. She doesn’t even grasp that everyone, everyone knows her name”.
