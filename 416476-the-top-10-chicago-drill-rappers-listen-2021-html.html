The top 10 Chicago drill rappers you should listen to in 2021

March 2024 · 5 minute read

The entertainment scene is getting better by the minute. More and more artists are coming up with different styles and genres. But none has gained as much traction as rap and hip-hop. Chicago drill rappers have defied conventions to release some of the most notable hits in 2021.

There is no doubt that Chicago is the home of musical talent. Many rappers who have come from the core of this city are sending shockwaves across American and the world over. Their style remains evergreen and refreshing, especially to their fans.

Top 10 Chicago drill rappers

Talk of rap and hip-hop, every artist has reinvented their style to ensure that fans are always entertained and remain connected for more music. When making a list of best Chicago drill rappers, you are bound to mention names that have not only been admired across America but the globe.

1. Pinqy Ring

Pingy Ring is one of Chicago's finest rap artists. He has released some of the best songs, which have won him awards and given him a name among the most influential American drill rappers. Some of his top songs are as follows:

  • Thank You
  • Dear Diary
  • Lipstick
  • I Cry
  • Trash It
  • Where Do We Go From Here
  • Dear Diary

2. TrenchMobb

TrenchMobb is a brand new Chicago drill rap group that is quickly making its mark in the entertainment industry. The young duo first starting gaining recognition in February 2017 with their first single, Take Off. The group is tearing up stages in the United States with no signs of slowing down.

3. Lil Durk

Lil Durk is among the pioneers of Chicago's hip-hop scene. Over the past decade, the sensational artist has been among the best melodic rap innovators, and his music has been cited among the best in the industry across America. His release from last summer Laugh Now Cry Later featuring Drake gave Lil Durk the much-desired recognition in the space.

5. Queen Key

Queen Key is a force to reckon with both in Chicago and across America. Even after becoming a mother to triplets back in 2020, she came back stronger in 2021. She has since released multiple singles like Ratchet, among others.

7. Joseph Chilliams

Joseph Chilliams remains a top-stage performer for lovers of drill music in Chicago. His latest release is a testimony of who he is in the Chicago music scene. From Fergie to his latest hit Wishlist and Taste it, his projects are primarily geared towards uniqueness and style.

8. Calboy

Calboy is another newcomer in the Chicago rap scene but has transformed the entire space. He rose to fame after releasing the 2018 hot tune Envy Me, which blew up on the internet. He is among the sensations to look up for in 2021, especially in terms of the collaborations with other superstars in the rap game.

9. Polo G

There would be no better way to represent the Chicago rap and hip-hop scene without the mention of Polo G. He landed the 20 Best Hip-Hop Albums of 2019 and has been termed the emerging leader of the new class of rappers in Chicago.
