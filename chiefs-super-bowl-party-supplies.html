While throwing a Taylor Swift Super Bowl party is one option, you can also keep the vibe entirely Chiefs-specific with tons of decorations and merchandise from Amazon. Plus, if you have a Prime Membership, you can count on everything to arrive at your doorstep before the big game, be it this Kansas City Chiefs Party Kit or this football field tablecloth .
