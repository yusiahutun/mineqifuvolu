Inside Justin Biebers incredible luxury watch collection: from his Rolex Day-Date II worn in P

August 2024 · 4 minute read
And it’s not just his songs that changed – Hailey Bieber’s 28-year-old husband also updated his wardrobe since. With such an astronomical bank balance, it’s no wonder he’s racked up an impressive luxury collection of designer fashion and accessories.

Bieber has sported some of the fanciest timepieces out there. Let’s take a look at the best ones to date …

1. Rolex Cosmograph Daytona

Soon after “Baby” became a global hit in 2010, the singer was spotted with a Rolex Cosmograph Daytona. It’s believed to be his first luxury timepiece, per Swiss Watch Expo. Bieber still sports it today, whether he’s having a casual day out, posing for an Instagram photo or attending events like Saturday Night Live 2020 and the Met Gala 2021.

The Cosmograph Daytona is regarded as one of the world’s most expensive and sought-after models favoured by superstars like Victoria Beckham and Michael Jordan.

Bieber’s 40mm version is set in 18k yellow gold with an engraved tachymetric scale, a champagne-coloured dial and an oyster bracelet. Its listed price is US$42,000 but it could go up to US$57,000, per Superwatchman.

2. Rolex Day-Date

The Day-Date is also known as the “President” watch, as it’s been worn by former American leaders like Lyndon B. Johnson, Gerald Ford, Bill Clinton and Donald Trump, per Rolex magazine.

Bieber has a model in 18k yellow gold with baguette-shaped diamonds on the champagne-coloured dial, as he showed off at his Believe movie’s world premiere in 2013, GQ reports. As its name suggests, the watch features a duo of calendar indications on the dial, one for the date and the other for the day. It is available on Jaztime for almost US$50,000.

3. Rolex Day-Date II

Bieber flaunted another Day-Date in the music video “Popstar” with DJ Khaled and rapper Drake.

The Rolex Day-Date II, a 41mm white-gold piece with diamonds all over the bezel and bracelet, truly sparkles on his wrist. The red dial with bedazzled Roman numerals makes it stand out even more. Superwatchman estimates its price tag to be about US$50,000.

4. Rolex Oyster Perpetual Datejust

Bieber also flashes his Rolex Oyster Perpetual Datejust in the “Popstar” music video, while rapping the lyrics, “Two, four, six, eight watches, factory, so they appreciate.”

One of those watches he appears to appreciate happens to be the Rolex Datejust 41, per The Truth About Watches.

It is a descendant of the original Oyster, the world’s first waterproof wristwatch launched in 1926. Accordingly, it features a waterproof Oystersteel case and white-gold hour markers shining on the dial with self-winding Perpetual rotor. Its price is around US$11,500, per the brand.

5. Patek Philippe Nautilus

Before his wedding in September 2018, Bieber rocked a dazzling Patek Philippe Nautilus when he and his then-fiancée Hailey went to see their pastor at Hillsong megachurch, per Insider.

It comes from the brand’s elegant and sporty Nautilus collection, but stands out from other models due to its custom set full of 255 white-gold and white round diamonds, per Esquire. Count them everywhere from the dial to the bezel and strap. It has a price tag of US$300,000, per Dmarge, beating all other models in his collection.

6. Audemars Piguet Royal Oak “Jumbo”

The “Love Yourself” singer treated himself to a vintage Audemars Piguet Royal Oak “Jumbo” watch as a wedding gift to himself, as he showed off on his Instagram. He then sported it at his wedding.

The ultra-thin 39mm descendants of the original 1972 watch are often nicknamed “Jumbo” for their oversized diameter at that time. The watch features an 18k yellow-gold set and bracelet. It also has a dark grey tapisserie dial as well as the self-winding Calibre, one of the world’s thinnest mechanical automatic rotor movements. The rare model was produced from 1978 to the mid 1980s with only roughly 1,000 pieces made, so its price tag isn’t small at all, around US$95,000, according to Superwatchman.

7. Cartier Santos-Dumont “Large”

Bieber clearly loves classic watches. He opted for a Cartier Santos-Dumont in a photo shoot for GQ’s May 2021 issue.

Originally released in 1904 as the first men’s wristwatch, it features Cartier’s iconic square-shaped case with a refined 18k rose-gold bezel. Its silver satin-brushed dial with sunray effect features Roman numerals, while its quartz movement is paired with an alligator leather band. It costs about US$6,800, per the brand.

8. Cartier Ballon Bleu

Confessing “I fell in love with the Cartier” in an Instagram post a few years ago, Bieber really shows it too. Besides the Santos-Dumont, he also sports the Ballon Bleu. He has been spotted with two Ballon Bleu models: an 18k rose-gold and an 18k white-gold model with a diamond-paved bezel and moonphase indicator. The 40mm models are worth around US$37,300 and US$40,700, respectively, per Cartier.

Introduced in 2007, the Ballon Bleu is suitable for both men and women. Another highlight is its protected crown featuring Cartier’s signature blue dial, hence its name that translates to “blue balloon”.
