Who is Frida Andersson's ex-husband, Jonathan Lourie?

June 2024 · 2 minute read

FRIDA Andersson is loved up with former footie ace Jamie Redknapp - and the pair are expecting their first child together.

Model Frida has been married before, to a well-known business tycoon. Here's more about her ex Jonathan Lourie.

Who is Jonathan Lourie?

 Jonathan Lourie is an American hedge fund manager.

Before his current role, Lourie worked at Morgan Stanley where he oversaw their convertible bond management practice.

He co-founded asset management firm Cheyne Capital, which is one of the largest hedge funds in Europe.

The tycoon was educated at Dartmouth College in Hanover, New Hampshire.

Lourie appeared on the Sunday Times Rich List in 2013, estimated to be worth £80million.

How long were the couple together?

It is not known how long the pair were together.

All we know is that Frida was pictured with Jonathan in 2013 and started dating Jamie in August 2020.

Jamie is also a divorcee, having split from his ex Louise in 2017 after 19 years of marriage following Louise's stint on Strictly Come Dancing.

How many children do they have?

Frida is three months pregnant with Jamie's baby and it is expected to arrive in November.

Frida shares four children with her ex-husband Jonathan and lives with them in a lavish mansion in West London.
