Where Is Lily Weinstein? Everything About Harvey Weinsteins Daughter

July 2024 · 5 minute read

Lily Weinstein, also known as Remy Lily Weinstein, is an American personality best known as the daughter of the notorious film producer Harvey Weinstein. She is the eldest of the four children of Harvey and his first wife, Eva Chilton.

Born into an elite family, Lily lived in all luxury and dignity since childhood, but things changed in 2017 after numerous allegations of sexual abuse against her father. Since then, she has kept a low profile and hardly appears in public. So where is she now? What does she do for a living?

In this article, we will explore some untold facts about her career, relationships, and more.

Lily Weinstein Belongs To A High-Class Family

Lily Weinstein, also known as Remy Lily Weinstein, was born on 20 February 1995 in New York City. As mentioned above, she is the daughter of her father Harvey Weinstein, and her mother Eva Chilton. She will be 27 years old in 2022.

Born and raised in a wealthy family, Remy had a luxurious childhood. In fact, she has an elite ancestry. Alongside her father, who once dominated the film industry as a producer, her grandparents also come from a strong background.

Her maternal grandfather, Thomas M. Chilton, was a successful legal entrepreneur and financial advisor with clients spread across Boston, Nantucket, and Willard counties. Lily’s great-grandfather, Edmund Randolph, was also the first Attorney General of the United States.

Her great-grandfather was also the founder of Hunton and Williams, the first global law firm. In addition, the family has been members of some of the most prestigious clubs, such as The Country Club in Brookline, Massachusetts.

Lily Weinstein Has Two Younger Sisters

She is not the only child of her parents. Instead, she has two younger siblings, Ruth Weinstein and Emma Weinstein. Emma was born on 2 February 1998 and the youngest sister Ruth was born on 31 October 2002.

Both sisters are now adults and, as any siblings should, they have a great relationship with each other. Unfortunately, however, they hardly appear together in public.

Both Of Remy Lily Weinstein’s Parents Have Been Married Twice

Both Lily’s dad and mum have been married twice. Her biological father Harvey and her mother Eve married in 1987. The former couple reportedly met in 1986 when Eve was working as Harvey’s assistant at his company Miramax. Shortly after their first meeting, they dated for a year before walking down the aisle.

The former duo was married for more than 15 years before their relationship broke down. They finally separated in 2003 and their divorce was finalized in 2004.

After the divorce, Lilia’s mother Eve married Sala Martirano. The couple is still married and lives a leisurely life in New York.

Harvey also married the English actress and fashion designer Georgina Chapman. They married in 2007 and divorced on 10 October 2017, shortly after news of his sexual harassment broke in the media. The divorce eventually took place in July 2021.

Her Father Was Convicted Of Sexual Harassment

Lily Weinstein’s father, Harvey Weinstein, was convicted of several sexual offenses. The first accusation of sexual assault against Harvey, dating back to the late 1970s, hit the media in October 2017. Soon, by the end of October, more than 80 women accused him of various forms of sexual harassment, which led to his dismissal from the company.

He was also accused of sexual harassment, assault, and rape, according to reports in the New York Times. Eventually, on 24 February 2020, he was indicted by a court on one count of sexual assault in the first degree and one count of rape in the third degree.

The former film producer was sentenced to 23 years imprisonment in the Vendée Correctional Institution.

His oldest daughters don’t speak to him. So I mean it’s horrific. The ripple effect of someone losing their freedom is beyond what any of us could comprehend. Where Is Lily Weinstein Now? Is She On Social Media?

Since the court convicted Lily’s father of sexual misconduct, Lily has avoided direct contact with the media. The last time the media reported on her was in October 2017 at her home in Los Angeles, shortly after she called emergency services in connection with her “suicidal” father.

She is also not visible on any social media such as Instagram and Twitter, making it even more difficult to track her whereabouts.
